When the classifier predicts zone Z, automatically query the Numogram oracle for AQ entries whose *syzygy chain* includes Z. Display the AQ word and its definition alongside the SHAP plot. For example, a track predicted Zone 7 (heavy/drone) might surface AQ terms like **"Grelak"** (Gate of the Abyss) or **"U contrasts"**. This turns music listening into a divinatory practice: *the track speaks through the oracle*.
