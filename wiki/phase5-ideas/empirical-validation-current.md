A meta-current: subject all claims to data-driven scrutiny. Publish null results (empty zones), use SHAP for explanation, cross-validate across modalities, let evidence reshape theory. Every skill proposal must answer: 'How will this be empirically validated?' If it can't, the idea stays in mythopoetic territory — which is fine, but we should know the boundary.
