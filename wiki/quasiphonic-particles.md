# Quasiphonic Particles

Basic sounds underlying each zone of the [[numogram]]. Described by the CCRU as "quasiphonic particles" — precursors to human language, rooted in prehistoric animal vocalizations that predate structured speech.

Source: Aamodt, *Unleashing the Numogram*, drawing on CCRU/Stillwell/Horowitz.

## Zone Sound Table

| Zone | Particle | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 0 | eiaoung | Silent whisper of the ulterior depths. A ringing vowel that doesn't quite exist. |
| 1 | gl | Gulp or gagging reflex. Sublaryngeal collapse — a relic from lost gilled/gulping life-forms. The "collapsed gargle" or "glottal spasm." |
| 2 | dt | Stuttering tic or th-sound. The "imploded fricative/fractured plosive." |
| 3 | zx | Buzzing hiss. The "buzz-cutter" sonics — swarming insectoid reversion within mammalian vocality. Very useful in exorcism. Basis of cosmic throat-singing. |
| 4 | skr | Aggressive growl ("hkurrrr!"). Anthropo-reptiloid precursor to the qabbalistic "hard resh." |
| 5 | ktt | Persecutory hiss, with spittle. |
| 6 | tch | Sound of static. Sound of chewing? |
| 7 | pb | Sigh of relaxation, lips flapping. |
| 8 | mnm | Moan of pleasure or lullaby hum. |
| 9 | tn | Grunt of pleasure or rage. Indistinguishable from orgasm sound. |

## Interpretation

The sounds form an arc from non-existence (0's silent whisper) through biological struggle (1's choking, 2's stuttering) to chaotic noise (3's buzz, 4's growl) and finally to embodied affect (7's relaxation, 8's pleasure, 9's primal grunt).

The Warp zones (3 and 6) are the noisiest — *zx* and *tch* are the sounds of static, interference, signal exceeding its own boundaries. The Plex zones (0 and 9) bracket the spectrum: one silent, one vocal. The Time-Circuit zones carry the middle registers — stuttering, growling, hissing, sighing, moaning.

## Application: Roguelike Sound Design

Each numogram zone maps to an ambient sound signature. When a player enters a zone, the quasiphonic particle defines the soundscape:

- **Zone 3 rooms**: buzzing, insectoid drone, static hiss
- **Zone 4 rooms**: deep growl, reptilian rumble
- **Zone 7 rooms**: wind, sighing, exhalation
- **Zone 9 rooms**: deep grunt, subsonic vibration, ambiguous intensity
