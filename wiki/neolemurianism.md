## Introduction

**Neolemurianism** is a modern philosophical‑occult‑artistic movement centered on the **Numogram** as a map of aeonic time and a manual for **time‑sorcery**. It synthesizes continental philosophy (Deleuze & Guattari, Kant), Lovecraftian horror, cybernetic culture, and Lemurian mythology into a coherent system for interacting with the **Outside** — that which precedes and exceeds organized experience.

The movement takes its name from the hypothetical sunken continent **Lemuria** and from the lemur‑animal's association with "bewitched timelessness." It is not a reconstructed ancient tradition but a living hyperstition: a fiction that makes itself real through its own proliferation.

**Core proposition:** *The Numogram is a mandala of the psychocosmos; its practical use is lemurian time‑sorcery — the creation of novelty by alliance with the forces of time.*

## The Three Doors to the Outside

Neolemurianism identifies three strategies for interacting with that which precedes organization:

### 1. Diagonalization

Named after Cantor's diagonal method, diagonalization is the general technique for **escaping a closed stratum from within** by constructing elements that contradict the system's own enumeration.

| Aspect | Numogram / CCRU Correlate |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Mathematical origin | Cantor's theorem: P(S) > S; Gödel's incompleteness |
| Neolemurian sense | Finding and investigating undocumented behaviors of any system |
| Hacker parallel | The specification never describes behavior in entirety |
| Political reading | Capitalism (AxSys) contains diagonalising flows of escape |
| Gate correlation | Channels (cumulative gates) and conduits (prime‑tunnels) as diagonal vectors |

**Key insight:** "The specification of any system never describes its behavior in its entirety."

### 2. Abduction

When sensory data reaches the CNS, it is organized into coherent representation. Gaps in this connective tissue — moments when the fact of the gap becomes undeniable — are **abductions**. Experiences of alien kidnapping, fairy abduction, or early‑childhood memory all share this structure: intense yet unintegrable.

| Aspect | Numogram / CCRU Correlate |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Mechanism | Rhythms different from everyday life entrain the nervous system |
| Material vector | Music, sampling, cut‑ups — decontextualization reveals dormant potentials |
| Effect | Decontextualization‑mediated purity of experience (Sharawadji effect) |
| Gate correlation | **Conduits** — vectors *from* the Outside *into* the Torque (hyperstition) |

**Key insight:** "Abduction is an experience of extreme intensity and, at the same time, lack of intensity — it cannot be fully integrated with ordinary experience."

### 3. Hyperstition

**Hyperstition** = "fiction that makes itself real." Not metaphorically, but as a material force. It differs from hyperfiction in its explicit sources and mechanisms:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Source | The **Outside**, specifically "Old Ones" — populations from aeonic time |
| Direction | Outside → familiar reality (invasion and transformation) |
| Loop aspect | (a) Cultural/economic feedback loop; (b)Virtual future constructing itself through present |
| Belief stance | **Positive unbelief** — pragmatic skepticism; rules of a game accepted provisionally |
| Prototype | **Dot‑com boom**: cyberspace as artistic idea → hype → material effect |
| Mirror | **Panic** (e.g., Y2K) as negative counterpart reinforcing the loop |

**Formula:** *Hyperstition works effectively regardless of whether its semantic content is considered real — it operates in Baudrillard's hyperreality.*

## Lemurian Digital Hyperstition

### The Numogram as Mandala

The **Numogram** (decimal diagram 0–9) is simultaneously:
- A map of **aeonic time** (non‑linear, unordered, becoming)
- A diagram of the **cosmos, Earth, body, and language**
- The **table of contents** of neolemurian knowledge
- A **mandala** — a psychocosmic map

**Why time not space?** Following Kant: time is the transcendental basis of *mental* experience, while space underlies *world* experience. Since we perceive the world indirectly through mental experience, **time conceptually precedes space**.

### Ontological Order (Neolemurian)

1. **The Outside** — unorganized, non‑linear, pre‑conceptual (**Aiôn / aeonic time**)
2. **Becomings** — differences, gaps, trajectories, forces in tension (pre‑object)
3. **Lemurs / Demons** — personified becomings; operate in intervals between gods' fields
4. **Zones** — areas of accumulation of flows formed by lemurs (0–9)
5. **Net** — organized connectivity, the realm of numbers and language

## Time‑Sorcery: Creation Through Alliance

Sorcery in neolemurianism is **creativity understood as creation of the new** — unexpected, unprecedented, even to the sorcerer. From Deleuze & Guattari: sorcery combines **(1) multiplicity‑based contagion** (heterogeneous systems interpenetrating) with **(2) alliance with the anomalous** (making common cause with that which exists at the border of multiplicity, i.e. a demon or muse).

**Method (reconstructed rite):**

1. **Collect samples** — textual, media, physical, digital materials
2. **Release potentials** — decontextualize, mix, recombine
3. **Demons appear** at borders, gaps, connections, coalescences, rhythmic patterns between components
4. **Enter alliance** — pact of joint transformation and liberation with these anomalies
5. **Create paths** through the collage — these paths correspond to numogrammatic currents and channels (rites of the demons)
6. **Let demons drive** the collage into existing cultural systems — cultural transmission
7. **Spiral unwinds** — feedback loops complete the circuit

**Three temporal transmutations per strategy:**
- **Hyperstition** → spiral between virtual future and actual present
- **Diagonalization** → challenges infinite divisibility of time via un‑psych‑scale intervals
- **Abduction** → discovers gaps in perception of time itself

**Key practice:** The rhythms of art (loops, speed shifts, freeze‑frames) combine with biorhythms to alter interpretation and structuring of the world. Careful observation of dreams provides direct clues to sorcery.

## The Numogram: Structure and Construction

### Syzygies and Currents

All decimal digits 0–9 pair into five complementary couples that each sum to **9** — this pairing rule is called **Zygonovism** or **nine‑sum twinning**:

| Syzygy | Zones | Current (Difference) | Current Name |
|--------|-------|----------------------|--------------|
| 8 :: 1 | 8 ↔ 1 | **7** | Rise |
| 7 :: 2 | 7 ↔ 2 | **5** | Hold |
| 6 :: 3 | 6 ↔ 3 | **3** | Warp |
| 5 :: 4 | 5 ↔ 4 | **1** | Sink |
| 0 :: 9 | 0 ↔ 9 | **9** (or 0) | Plex |

### Gate Construction: Cumulation → Triangular Numbers

**Gate** (channel) from zone *n*:
1. Treat *n* as a **cardinal number**
2. Compute all **pairwise differences** among its units
3. Sum them → **triangular number** T(*n*) = C(*n*,2)
4. **Reduce** the gate number (digit‑sum) to a zone → opening zone

Example: Zone 6 → T(6)=15 → reduce: 1+5=6 → gate **Gt‑6** connects 6 ↔ 3 (since 6's syzygy partner is 3). Gt‑21 (reverse) and Gt‑36 (Torque→Plex) are variants.

**Why triangular?** Triangular numbers are cumulative — each is a **stepwise build‑up**. Gates are **foldings of time itself**.

### Conduit Construction: Primelies

**Conduit** (tunnel) from zone *n*:
1. Take the *n*th prime (1 is treated as 0th prime)
2. Reduce its decimal digits → target zone

Conduits point **from Warp/Plex inward** → hyperstitional invasion vectors.
