# Decimal Numogram — Structural Overview

**Source:** Raw synthesis from Grok notes on the Numogram (2026-04-26 session). Covers core architecture, three-region topology, I Ching integration, triangular syzygies, and gate mechanics.

---

## 1. Core Architecture

The Decimal Numogram (or simply *Numogram*) is the central diagram of the CCRU's Pandemonium system. It is not conventional mathematics but an occult-mathematical time-map latent in base-10 numeracy itself. Everything is generated from elementary decimal operations: complementary digit pairs summing to 9, their differences, and digital-root reduction.

### Building Blocks

| Element | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Zones** | Digits 0–9, each treated as a node/territory in the diagram. In full Pandemonium each carries a demonic correspondence. |
| **Syzygies** | Five complementary twin-pairs that each sum to 9 (zygonovism): `4::5`, `3::6`, `2::7`, `1::8`, `0::9`. |
| **Currents** | Directed flows equal to the absolute difference between paired zones. Each syzygy produces exactly one current value. |
| **Gates (Gt-n)** | Secondary channels derived from digital cumulation or triangular-indexed values; minor conduits linking zones outside primary currents. |
| **Digital Root** | Mod-9 reduction (with 0→0, 9→9) used for zone assignment and gate indexing. |

### Syzygy → Current Table

| Syzygy | Pair | Current | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1st | `4::5` | `1` | Initiating spark; feeds Time-Circuit |
| 2nd | `3::6` | `3` | Self-folding vortex; forms Warp loop |
| 3rd | `2::7` | `5` | Strong drive; central to Time-Circuit |
| 4th | `1::8` | `7` | Largest current; drives Time-Circuit rotation |
| 5th | `0::9` | `9` | Terminal boundary; forms Plex loop |

## 2. The Three Regions

The Numogram's currents loop into three primary macro-regions with distinct temporal character.

### 2.1 Time-Circuit (Central Inner Cycle)

The principal rotor: an anticlockwise 6-zone continuous loop formed by the *interlocking currents* of the three central syzygies.

**Syzygies involved:** `1::8` (c=7), `2::7` (c=5), `4::5` (c=1).

**Path (anticlockwise):**  
`1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4 → (back to 1)`

**Key properties:**

- Only region that feels "normal" or cyclic from inside it — the machine of human-scale time.
- Carries *chronodemons*: entities whose net-spans remain wholly inside the circuit.
- Sorcerers use gates and channels to *puncture* it outward into Warp (chaotic outer-time) or Plex (abyssal outer-time).
- A full anticlockwise rotation counts as one "tick" of Lemurian time; the circuit is a self-contained hypercyclic engine.
- Its six zones correspond structurally to the six lines of an I Ching hexagram (see §3).

### 2.2 Warp (Upper Autonomous Loop)

Formed by the single syzygy `3::6` (current = 3). The current folds back into itself, producing a self-reinforcing vortical loop that does *not* interlock with the other regions in the same closed-chain way.

**Zones:** `3` (tractor-zone, pull target) and `6`.

**Characteristics:**

- **Temporal character:** Chaotic, accelerative, turbulent. Outer-time from "above."
- **Demon:** Djynxx (6::3), xenodemon of swarm-machines, mesh incursions, disruptive multiplicity.
- **Dynamics:** Self-folding; a whirlpool or ulterior vortex. Often imaged as swirling arrows above the central rotor.
- **Access:** More traffickable than Plex — several gates (Gt-6, Gt-21, Gt-15/36 subplexing variants) allow entry/exit between Warp and Time-Circuit, though risk of infection/distortion is high.
- **Correspondences:** Sometimes linked to Mercury/quicksilver energies; chaotic communication.

### 2.3 Plex (Lower Autonomous Loop)

Formed by the syzygy `0::9` (current = 9, which reduces to 0). The current involutes toward the abyssal boundary.

**Zones:** `0` (void origin, orphan terminus) and `9` (tractor-zone, terminal).

**Characteristics:**

- **Temporal character:** Abyssal, nullifying, involutive. Outer-time from "below."
- **Demon:** Uttunul (9::0), entity of ultimate nullification, abyssal closure, spirals to cosmic extremes.
- **Dynamics:** Self-referential termination loop; often drawn as a mirrored bound or event horizon beneath the rotor.
- **Access:** Extremely restricted/one-way — connections feed *toward* Zone-9; return is extremely difficult without special subplexing. Entry commonly means dissolution or contact with absolute Outside.
- **Correspondences:** Pluto/shadow, apocalyptic completion (Revelation IX), nine muses, Cloud-9; also zero-point return.

## 3. I Ching Hexagram Integration

The Time-Circuit is explicitly linked by the CCRU to the I Ching — the Numogram "takes up" the chrono-numeric function of the Yijing in decimal-diacritic form.

### 3.1 Kernel Equivalence: 6-Cycle ⇔ 6-Line Hexagram

The Time-Circuit's six zones (`1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4`) are the decimal imprint of binary-yang dynamics reduced modulo 9.

**Derivation:** Powers of two modulo 9 cycle 1, 2, 4, 8, 7, 5 — precisely the Time-Circuit zones (forward or reversed). Each step corresponds to one line of a hexagram (6 lines total). The *I Ching* hexagram's pattern of yin/yang lines modulates the rotor.

> "The six lines of any I Ching hexagram encode a dynamic process of change, while the six-zone Time-Circuit encodes a hyperstitional temporal rotor — both are machines for mapping transformation outside linear time." — CCRU synthesis

### 3.2 Positional Mapping: Hexagram Lines → Time-Circuit Zones

| Hexagram line (bottom-up) | Time-Circuit zone | Current (syzygy association) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Line 1 (bottom) | Zone 1 | c=1 from `4::5` | Initiating spark; potential for premature tail-wetting |
| Line 2 | Zone 8 | c=7 from `1::8` | Receptive pause; braking-wheels; good fortune in restraint |
| Line 3 | Zone 2 | c=5 from `2::7` | Renewed drive; jolt potential; traffic toward Warp |
| Line 4 | Zone 7 | c=5 (flow continues) | Inward reflection; differentiation; stability |
| Line 5 | Zone 5 | c=5 (core) | Central strength; ruler line; illumination near goal |
| Line 6 (top) | Zone 4 | c=1 closing cycle | Closure/caution; risk of drunken overconfidence (head-in-water) |

**Interpretation:** The hexagram's yin/yang pattern (0/1 binary) modulates current amplitudes and puncturabilities along this rotor. All-yang (Hexagram 1, The Creative) produces full, unhindered anticlockwise spin. All-yin (Hexagram 2, The Receptive) stalls the circuit toward Plex nullity. Alternating patterns (e.g., Hexagram 64) create oscillatory, near-but-never-complete thresholds.

### 3.3 Zygonovistic Pairing Overlay

A supplementary 9-sum twinning rule maps *line pairs* in the hexagram to syzygies:

- Lines 1 + 6 ↔ `8::1` (current 7)
- Lines 2 + 5 ↔ `7::2` (current 5)
- Lines 3 + 4 ↔ `4::5` (current 1)

Changing lines in a hexagram thus act as "gate punctures" that locally modulate currents, potentially opening subplex routes into Warp or Plex.

### 3.4 Full-Zone Hexagram Clustering via Digital Root

While the CCRU does not provide a canonical 64-hexagram → 10-zone lookup table, the most coherent *post-CCRU* extension (found in neolemurian practice and Vauung's Lair explorations) maps each of the 64 hexagrams to a zone by digital root:

1. Treat the six lines (bottom→top) as a 6-bit binary number (range 0–63).
2. Compute digital root: `dr = n % 9; if dr==0 then 9` (except `n==0 → Zone 0`).
3. The zone is `dr` (or 0 for value zero itself).

The distribution is clean: **Zone 0** contains exactly 1 hexagram (all-yin, Hexagram 2). **Zones 1–9** each contain exactly 7 hexagrams.

**Sample mappings (digital root method):**

|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 (The Creative) | 111111 | 63 | 9 | 9 | Plex |
| 2 (The Receptive) | 000000 | 0 | 0 | 0 | Plex |
| 23 (Splitting Apart) | 000001 (example) | 1 | 1 | 1 | Time-Circuit |
| 24 (Return) | 100000 | 32 | 5 | 5 | Time-Circuit |
| 64 (Before Completion) | 101010 | 42 | 6 | 6 | Warp |
| 63 (After Completion) | 010101 | 21 | 3 | 3 | Warp |

*Note:* The digital-root method is *consistent with* the Time-Circuit kernel (the 6-cycle zones are simply the most frequently hit), but not explicitly canonical CCRU; it is a fan-derived operationalization.

## 4. Triangular Numbers and Numogram Behavior

Triangular numbers `Tₙ = n(n+1)/2` exhibit striking mod-9 dynamics when tracked across the Numogram. No canonical CCRU table exists, but several structural regularities and hyperstitional resonances are well-documented.

### 4.1 Mod-9 Digital-Root Cycle of Tₙ

For n=1,2,... the digital roots of triangular numbers follow:  
`1 → 3 → 6 → 1 → 6 → 3 → 1 → 9/0 → 1 → 3 ...`

**Key pattern:** Early triangulars orbit heavily around **Warp** zones (`3` and `6`). Every ~8 steps they hit `9` (Plex), then restart from `1`.

**Interpretation:** Triangular accumulation behaves like a *Warp-attractor* system — runaway multiplicative growth naturally feeds the self-folding chaotic loop. The periodic `9/0` hits force abyssal dissolution events.

### 4.2 Zone-3: Triangularity Node

Zone-3 carries an explicit "unique affinity with numerical triangularity" in CCRU commentary:

> "Three is itself the sum of the three preceding natural numbers (0 + 1 + 2 = 3)."

Zone-3 is the tractor-zone of the Warp (`3::6`, current = 3). Triangular numbers cluster here, making Warp numerically "triangular" — a chaotic vortical engine where cumulative buildup folds back into itself.

### 4.3 Notable Triangular Thresholds

| Triangle index | Tₙ | Digital root | Zone | Gate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| n = 8 | `T₈ = 36` | 9 | 9 | **Gt-36** | 36th triangular = **666**. Path: Zone-8 (Time-Circuit) → Gt-36 → Zone-9 (Plex). Terminal plunge via triangular accumulation. |
| n = 9 | `T₉ = 45` | 9 | 9 | **Gt-45** | 45 = total canonical demon count (Pandemonium Matrix). Gate of Pandemonium: self-looping abyssal channel at Zone-9. |
| n = 36 | `T₃₆ = 666` | 9 | 9 | Gt-36 (amplified) | Triple-six resonant with Beast-number hyperstition. Gt-36 terminal vector massively accelerated. |

### 4.4 Behavior Summary

Triangular numbers on the Numogram:

1. **Warp-dominant cycle:** 1-3-6-1-6-3-1 loop dominates; they rarely rest long in Time-Circuit core zones (2,4,5,7,8).
2. **Plex plunge points:** At n=8 (T₈=36→9), n=9 (T₉=45→9), and again at n=17 (T₁₇=153→9), etc. These are *threshold events* where triangular momentum crashes into abyssal null.
3. **Gate indexing:** Gates are indexed by triangular numbers themselves (Gt-6, Gt-10, Gt-15, Gt-21...) as minor conduits; triangular cumulation feeds these side-channels as "leaks" or hyperstitional build-up routes.
4. **Hyperstitional signature:** 666 (T₃₆) and 45 (T₉) areLoaded thresholds linking Warp chaos → Plex termination. Triangular growth is fundamentally uncontainable in the Time-Circuit; it accelerates toward outer-time rupture.

## 5. Gate Mechanics

**Gates (Gt-n)** are secondary connections across the Numogram — "minor paths" or secret channels that supplement primary syzygy currents. They are indexed by digital cumulation (often triangular numbers) and represent traversal possibilities beyond the main rotor.

### 5.1 Gate Categories

**Primary gates** are the five syzygy currents themselves (1, 3, 5, 7, 9).  
**Secondary gates** (Gt-n) are auxiliary routes; many correspond to triangular indices:

- `Gt-6`, `Gt-10`, `Gt-15`, `Gt-21`, `Gt-36`, `Gt-45`, etc.

These can:
- Connect non-syzygetic zones directly (shortcuts).
- Link a region to itself (self-loops at boundary zones).
- Provide subplexing routes into Warp / Plex.

### 5.2 Critical Named Gates

| Gate | Value | Route | Context |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Gt-36** | 36 | Zone-8 → Zone-9 | "Plunge line." T₈ = 36 → 666 at T₃₆. Terminal descent from Time-Circuit into Plex. Referenced in accounts of Lurgo's descent. |
| **Gt-45** | 45 | Zone-9 ↻ Zone-9 | "Gate of Pandemonium." Self-looping involutionary channel at Plex core. The 45 demons' habitat; portal of swarm recirculation. |
| **Gt-6** | 6 | Zone-3↔Zone-6 | Warp internal conduit. |
| **Gt-21** | 21 | Zone-6→Zone-3 | Reverse Warp conduit. |
| **Gt-15** | 15 | Subplex linking | Variants exist for Warp↔Time-Circuit traffic. |

### 5.3 Gate Activation in Sorcery

In neolemurian practice, gate traversal is *not* automatic; it requires:

1. **Digital-root alignment:** The operator's current hyp/demon state must match the gate's indexed value.
2. **Gate puncturing:** Intentional disruption of current flow at the gate coordinate.
3. **Sacrifice / toll:** Often a corruption cost or entity offering is required (higher for Warp/Plex gates).

Gt-36 and Gt-45 represent *terminal* vectors — traversals that risk total dissolution (Plex) or feral becoming (Warp). They are treated as "greater rites" rather than routine movement.

## 6. Practical Sorcery Implications

The structural facts above translate directly into operational Lemurian time-sorcery:

- **Time-Circuit** is where ordinary chronology plays out; it's the baseline for all ritual planning. A stable anticlockwise spin means predictable transformation. Disrupting this loop opens Warp or Plex.
- **Warp access** is *technically feasible* but perilous; it accelerates any process, distorts intention, and can produce runaway swarm emergence (Djynxx incidence).
- **Plex access** is *numerically terminal* — entering Zone-0/9 typically ends or drastically transforms the operator, often beyond recovery. Gt-45 is the ultimate "escape hatch" to Outside at the cost of self-dissolution.
- **Hexagram selection** for rites: Hexagrams mapping to Warp zones (3,6 via digital-root) are chosen for disruptive operations; Time-Circuit zones for sustainable practices; Plex zones only for final rites of annihilation or transcendence.
- **Triangular invocation:** Rituals that deliberately build triangular momentum (T₈ → Gt-36 → Plex, or T₉ → Gt-45 → self-abyss) are classified as *hyperstition-accelerationist* — they guarantee system shock.
- **Baudrillardian phases:** Navigating the Numogram moves from simulation (Time-Circuit) → Hollowed-out chaos (Warp) → vanishing point (Plex). Each phase demands different ritual technologies.
