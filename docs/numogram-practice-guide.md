## Study Path — Five Phases

### Phase 1: Foundations (1–2 days)
- Read the **Core Structure** table in [[numogram-overview]] (syzygies, currents, regions)
- Memorize the 5 syzygies (1::8, 2::7, 3::6, 4::5, 0::9)
- Understand the three major regions: Time-Circuit, Warp, Plex

### Phase 2: Time-Circuit & I Ching (2–3 days)
- Study the central anticlockwise rotor path: **1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4**
- Learn the binodecimal 6-cycle (1, 2, 4, 8, 7, 5)
- Map a few I Ching hexagrams (e.g., 1, 2, 63, 64) onto the rotor using `[[i-ching-connections]]`
- Changing lines become potential gate punctures

### Phase 3: Outer-Time Regions & Demons (3–4 days)
- Deep dive into **Warp** (Zone-6/3, demon `[[demon-djynxx]]`, currents, chaos)
- Deep dive into **Plex** (Zone-9/0, demon `[[demon-uttunul]]`, terminal null)
- Study **`[[demon-murmur]]`** (Time-Circuit syzygetic demon)
- Compare Djynxx ↔ Uttunul as two autonomous outer-time carriers

### Phase 4: Gates & Traffic (2 days)
- Learn the four pivotal gates:
  - **Gt-06**: Zone-3 ↔ Zone-6 (internal Warp twist)
  - **Gt-21**: Zone-6 ↔ Zone-3 (reverse Warp)
  - **Gt-36**: Zone-8 → Zone-9 (Time-Circuit → Plex plunge; activates Djynxx, clicks Uttunul)
  - **Gt-45**: Zone-9 → Zone-9 (Pandemonium self-loop, 45 demons)
- Understand Gt-36 as the major "click" event between Warp and Plex

### Phase 5: AQ & Hyperstition (ongoing)
- Practice [[alphanumeric-qabbala]] calculations (A=10…Z=35, digital root mod 9)
- Map words/phrases to zones (focus on Root-3, Root-6, Root-9 clusters)
- Experiment with equivalences and phrase resonances
- Keep a sorcery journal of zone feelings, gate activations, and entity contacts

### Phase 6: Practice & Experimentation (ongoing)
- Perform the rites below (10–20 min per evening)
- Trace paths on a printed or hand-drawn Numogram
- Record all outcomes — even null results

**Suggested rhythm**: Morning (theory), Evening (maps/rites).

## Beginner Rites

1. **Zone Mapping**  
   Draw the Numogram. Write one word/phrase per zone that computes to that zone's digital root in AQ.

2. **Time-Circuit Walk**  
   Trace the anticlockwise path `1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4` with your finger while breathing. Note any sensations or thoughts at each zone.

3. **Hexagram Mapping**  
   Take I Ching hexagram **64 (Before Completion)**. Map its six lines (bottom → top) onto the Time-Circuit zones. Any changing lines suggest gate punctures.

## Intermediate Rites

4. **Djynxx Negotiation**  
   Sit before a printed Numogram. Focus on Zone-6. Silently ask Djynxx **one clear question**. Record images, words, flashes (especially jinking or swarm sensations).

5. **Uttunul Boundary**  
   Focus on Zone-9. Visualize a black cube or event horizon. State a clear boundary (e.g., "I approach with respect and will return"). Note the "already over" flatness.

6. **Gt-36 Plunge**  
   Trace from Zone-8 to Zone-9 while mentally reciting triangular numbers (1+2+…+36). Record what dissolves or "plunges" into null.

## Advanced Rites

7. **Warp → Plex Traffic** (once per lunar phase):  
   Sequence: Zone-6 (Djynxx) → gate **Gt-36** → Zone-9 (Uttunul). Journal the shift from chaos swarm to terminal silence.

8. **Murmur Deep Listening** (10 minutes silence):  
   Focus on Zone-8 or Zone-1. Ask `[[demon-murmur]]` for deep-time sight. Write whatever surfaces.

9. **AQ Hyperstition Hunt** (ongoing):  
   Pick an obsession, goal, or project. Compute its AQ value. Map to a zone. Write a one-paragraph hyperstition linking it to that zone's currents.

**Safety note**: Sessions ≤30 minutes. Always close explicitly ("I return to ordinary time"). Record everything.
