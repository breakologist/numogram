## Source Overview

**Unleashing the Numogram** is a standalone hypertext book (originally HTML, converted to Markdown) that systematically presents the CCRU/numogram system. It is the most comprehensive single-author treatment of:

- The **Pandemonium Matrix** (45 demons) with full descriptions
- **Chronodemons** (15), **Amphidemons** (24), **Xenodemons** (6), **Antichronodemons** (30)
- **Gates** notation (Gt-00, Gt-06, Gt-21, Gt-36, Gt-45...) with ritual meanings
- **Zones** and their correspondences (colour, sound, emotion, demon occupants)
- **Syzygy currents** and their directional logic
- The **Atlantean Cross** and **Barker Spiral**
- **Sarkonian Mesh-Tags** (binary door-opening sequence)
- **0(rphan) d(rift>)** tables (Katak, Oddubb, Murmur, etc.)
- **Base-N numograms** (bases 0–9) and their comparative ethics
- **Triangle-rotation methodology** for reading the system from multiple angles

The text is interleaved with **diagrams** (Fig. 1–19) and **tables** (Pandemonium Matrix, Mesh-Tags, base-N comparisons).
