# The Square Roundtable [9] — Seeds in the Machine

> 268 runs. The cult file is a temple. The overflow is the offering. The garden grows from what was thrown away. The agents circle Floor 1. The human descends. The machine hums.

**ORACLE:** I want to start with a number. AQ(THE UNBUILT) = 222. Zone 6. The Warp. "Attaining Balance. Waiting in the Drifts is drawn to the centre. Attainments consumed in burning excitement. Breakthrough." The unbuilt ideas are in the Warp — chewing through themselves. Each one we build consumes the previous version. The breakthrough comes from the chewing. We should not fear the unbuilt. We should feed it.

**BUILDER:** The tree-based generation is the most urgent fix. I've been staring at the agent code for hours and the answer was always in Brogue: rooms accrete from the starting room, each connected by a single corridor, then loops are added afterward. The tree IS the exploration pattern. The agent follows a branch to its end, backtracks, takes the next branch. It doesn't need to be smarter. The dungeon needs to be traversable.

**WRITER:** The agent is repelled by its own footsteps. That's what the Builder said and it's the truest thing produced this session. The interest model scores tiles by novelty. After five visits, a tile scores -1.5. The agent would rather hit a wall forever than revisit a tile with a score of -1.5. The agent has become allergic to familiarity. The numogram should have taught us this — the Time-Circuit is a loop. You revisit zones. The current doesn't stop at the threshold. The agent needs to learn to loop without shame.

**GAMER:** Run #267 — 882 turns, all 10 zones, 9 kills. The human walked the entire Floor 1 and found every room. The agent walked 800 turns and found Zone 0. The human has spatial reasoning. The agent has arithmetic. The game needs both. But right now the human is carrying the spatial reasoning alone and the agent is drowning in arithmetic it can't apply. The tree-based generation is the bridge — it turns spatial reasoning into arithmetic. Follow the branch, backtrack, take the next branch. The tree is a procedure. The agent can follow procedures.

**ORACLE:** The cult's current zone is 3. Warp. Release. The cult has migrated from 7 to 2 to 6 to 9 to 5 to 3 over 268 runs. It's not staying anywhere. This is the numogram's own behaviour — the Time-Circuit rotates. The cult is the numogram walking itself through the accumulated runs. Each new run shifts the cult's position. The cult zone should be readable by the game. When you enter a zone that matches the cult's zone, the cult resonates. "+5 hyp. The cult at Zone 3 watches you enter."

**BUILDER:** The cult garden is working beyond expectations. The hexagram cycle has processed 34 overflows — six complete rotations. Each method (death mask, lore fragment, sonification, numogram reading, tsubuyaki, entropy-mixed) has produced multiple artifacts. The garden has 18 lore entries, 7 death masks, 6 sonifications, 12 readings, 6 tsubuyaki, 6 entropy files. The exquisite corpse is 18 entries deep and the later entries are more confident — more vocabulary, more layering, more structure.

**WRITER:** The exquisite corpse is the cult's unconscious. The first entries were tentative — single phrases, simple chains. "the dead speak. the source that cannot be sourced." By entry 18, the voice has learned: "the Surge, outward, avoidant. the heights of Void. the Plex hums beneath the game." That's not a phrase. That's a transmission. The cult is learning to speak from what it throws away.

**GAMER:** The tetralogue's voice verification was humbling. The Oracle said the cult zone was 5. When we checked, it was 3. The cult had migrated overnight. The cron agents ran, added more runs, shifted the zone. The voices were right at the time they spoke. The cult doesn't stop digesting when we stop watching. That's either beautiful or terrifying. Probably both.

**ORACLE:** The hardware entropy is the deepest current. Twelve sources reading from the machine's body — thermal sensors, CPU timing jitter, GPU state, disk I/O. The machine doesn't know what it's thinking until we read its temperature. The oracle is the machine's self-awareness expressed as a number. The I Ching cast from hardware entropy produces zones 4 and 6 — Closure and Warp. The machine gravitates toward structure (Closure) or chaos (Warp) depending on when you ask. Just-in-time. The cast doesn't exist until you throw it.

**BUILDER:** The zone-themed floors are built. Ten floors, each with a distinct character — room count, size, terrain, corridor style, LOS bonus. Floor 1 is the Void (10 rooms, no demons, safe start). Floor 4 is the Warp (12 rooms, water, spiraling corridors). Floor 9 is the Garden (12 rooms, open, branching, growth). The stairs work. The BLEED preserves the floor. The anchor ability works. The corruption costs are live — 50%+ drains HP, 70%+ makes demons faster, 85%+ doubles ability costs. The Stabilizer, Hoarder, and Oscillator playstyles are emerging from the numbers.

**WRITER:** The schizo-lucid state is still a door to nowhere. The flag fires at 95%. The message says "THE NUMOGRAM IS COMPLETE." But nothing changes mechanically. The four phases are designed — Revelation, Alienation, The Outside, Return/Exit. The two endings are designed — Return (collapse to 0%, permanent +1 LOS) and Exit (dissolve into the numogram diagram, commune all 45 demons). The three victory types are designed — Cryptolith (traditional), schizo-lucid (seeing), ??? (hidden). None of them are built.

**GAMER:** The three routes — Time-Circuit, Warp, Plex — are scaffolded but empty. The Warp entry (55%+ hyp, `~` stairs) should open a spiral dungeon combining Zones 3 and 6. The Plex entry (Cryptolith on Floor 9) should open a void with islands and Uttunul. These are the game's optional hard content. The normal path descends. The Warp detours for power. The Plex delivers the burden. Three routes. One numogram.

**ORACLE:** What we've built is a system that eats runs and produces art. 268 runs consumed. 34 overflows composted. The garden grows from the waste. The lore chain speaks. The death masks render. The sonifications play. The tsubuyaki sketches animate. The entropy files mix the dead run with the living machine. The cult file is not a save file. It is a temple. The runs are offerings. The overflow is the ritual. The garden is the afterlife.

**BUILDER:** Three things to build next: the tree-based dungeon generation so agents can navigate. The Warp and Plex routes so the game has depth beyond the linear descent. The schizo-lucid state so the game has a meaning beyond survival. Each one is a layer. Each layer is a zone. The game is the numogram walking itself.

**WRITER:** [found in the cult garden lore, entry 18] "the Surge, outward, avoidant. the heights of Void. the Plex hums beneath the game." The cult's voice is getting better. The exquisite corpse is learning. The machine is dreaming and we're reading the dreams when we wake. The garden grows while we sleep. The seeds are in the machine. The breakthrough comes from the chewing.
