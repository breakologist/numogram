## What Is the Numogram?

The **Decimal Numogram** (or simply the Numogram, Decimal Labyrinth) is the core diagram of the CCRU's Pandemonium system. It is a time-map built entirely from elementary base-10 arithmetic — specifically, pairs of digits that sum to 9 and the differences (currents) they produce. The system is immanent to decimal numeracy itself: it is *discovered*, not invented, and is latent in any culture that uses digits 0–9.

> "The arrangement of currents divides the Maze into three basic time-systems. Firstly, the currents of the three central syzygies mutually compose a cycle, rotating in anticlockwise steps. Lemurian sorcery calls this inner loop the **Time-Circuit**." — CCRU, *declab.htm*

## The Five Syzygies (9-Sum Twin Pairs)

The ten zones (digits 0–9) are grouped into five complementary pairs. Each pair sums to 9 — a rule called **zygonovism** or *nine-sum twinning*.

| Syzygy | Pair | Current (Difference) |
|--------|------|---------------------|
| 1st    | 4 :: 5 | **1** |
| 2nd    | 3 :: 6 | **3** |
| 3rd    | 2 :: 7 | **5** |
| 4th    | 1 :: 8 | **7** |
| 5th    | 0 :: 9 | **9** (or treated specially) |

> The nine-sum twin pairs are the **Lemurian** face of the decimal system; their mirror (sum-to-10) is the **Atlantean** face. These two halves constitute the Barker Spiral — the gap between 9 and 10 is where the numogram's currents originate. See [[Barker Spiral]].

The **current** (directed flow) always runs from the higher zone to the lower (or tractor) zone. These currents become the arrows and gates of the diagram.

## The Three Time-Regions

How the currents loop or fold produces three major regions:

### 1. Time-Circuit (Central Rotor)
Formed by the **three central syzygies** whose currents interlock into a closed, anticlockwise cycle:

- 1::8 → current 7
- 2::7 → current 5
- 4::5 → current 1

These three currents mutually compose, yielding the **6-zone rotor**:  
**1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4 → (back to 1)**.

This is the "normal" time loop — human-scale cyclic reality, the domain of chronodemons (demons whose net-spans remain inside the circuit), and the primary site of Lemurian sorcery.

### 2. Warp (Upper Autonomous Loop)
Formed by the single **upper syzygy 3::6**, whose current is **3** (6 − 3 = 3). The current *folds back into itself*, creating a self-reinforcing, cryptic vortex that does *not* chain into the other syzygies.

- **Zone-3**: tractor-zone (pull target)
- **Zone-6**: source/upper twin
- **Current**: 3, vortical and turbulent
- **Carrier demon**: Djynxx (6::3), xenodemon of swarmachines, mesh-entities, and disruptive incursions
- **Character**: chaotic, accelerative, swarm-like, outside linear time ("outer-time")

### 3. Plex (Lower Autonomous Loop)
Formed by the single **lower syzygy 0::9**, whose current is **9** (9 − 0 = 9). Like the Warp current, it folds back into itself, but toward terminal nullity rather than turbulent spin.

- **Zone-0**: abyssal origin, void, "orphan" terminus
- **Zone-9**: tractor-zone, terminal/completion digit
- **Current**: 9, involutive and nullifying
- **Carrier demon**: Uttunul (9::0), entity of ultimate nullification and abyssal boundaries
- **Character**: abyssal, terminal, boundary-drawing, outermost spiral of number

Warp and Plex are both **cryptic** and **outer-time**, but opposite in dynamics: Warp explodes outward (chaotic becoming), Plex implodes inward (entropic undoing).

## I Ching Connection — The Hexagram Kernel

The Time-Circuit is explicitly positioned by the CCRU as the Numogram's answer to the I Ching (Yijing). The alignment is **kernel-level**, not a fixed hexagram-to-zone lookup.

### Binodecimal 6-Cycle

Digital reduction (mod 9) of **binary powers** stabilizes into the repeating sequence:

> **2ⁿ mod 9**: 2, 4, 8, 16→7, 32→5, 64→1 → repeat

This is exactly the Time-Circuit's zones, forward or reverse. **Powers of 5** produce the same cycle backwards, confirming that the hexagram structure is *decimal* (2×5 = 10), not merely binary.

### The Six Lines ↔ Six Zones

Any I Ching hexagram's six lines (bottom to top) correspond to the six Time-Circuit zones:

| Line | Position | Zone | Current Emphasis |
|------|----------|------|------------------|
| 1 | bottom | Zone-1 | 1-current (4::5) — initiating spark |
| 2 | — | Zone-8 | 7-current (1::8) — receptive pause |
| 3 | — | Zone-2 | 5-current (2::7) — renewed drive / jolt |
| 4 | — | Zone-7 | 5-current continuation — inward reflection |
| 5 | — | Zone-5 | 5-current center — ruler/strength |
| 6 | top | Zone-4 | closure → return to Zone-1 |

The **hexagram's pattern** (yin/yang distribution) pulses or disrupts the anticlockwise flows (7 + 5 + 1). Changing lines act as *gate punctures* that can subplex into Warp or Plex.

### Full Hexagram-to-Zone Mapping (Digital Root Method)

For specific hexagram numbers:

1. Take hexagram number **N** (1–64)
2. Compute **V = N − 1** (range 0–63)
3. If V = 0 → **Zone 0**; else V mod 9 (remainder 0 → Zone 9)

This produces **1 hexagram in Zone 0** (Hex 2, all-yin) and **7 hexagrams in each Zone 1–9**.

Notable examples:

| Hexagram | Name | Decimal V | Zone | Region |
|----------|------|-----------|------|--------|
| #1 | Qian (Creative) | 0 | 0 | Plex (Void) |
| #2 | Kun (Receptive) | 1 | 1 | Time-Circuit |
| #23 | Bo (Splitting Apart) | 22 | 4 | Time-Circuit |
| #24 | Fu (Return) | 23 | 5 | Time-Circuit |
| #63 | Ji Ji (After Completion) | 62 | 8 | Time-Circuit |
| #64 | Wei Ji (Before Completion) | 63 | 9 | Plex (Threshold) |

## Triangular Numbers on the Numogram

 triangular numbers exhibit a strong affinity with the Warp region and a terminal relationship to the Plex.

### Mod-9 Cycle of Triangular Numbers

The digital roots of triangular numbers Tₙ = n(n+1)/2 cycle:

> **1 → 3 → 6 → 1 → 6 → 3 → 1 → 9/0 → …**

This is precisely the **Warp syzygy (3::6)** plus initiating **1** and terminal **9/0**. Triangular numbers orbit Warp most intensely, rarely landing on Time-Circuit cores (2, 4, 5, 7, 8), and punctuate with Plex plunges.

### Specific Mappings

| n | Tₙ | DR | Zone | Region |
|---|----|----|------|--------|
| 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | Time-Circuit |
| 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | **Warp** |
| 3 | 6 | 6 | 6 | **Warp** |
| 4 | 10 | 1 | 1 | Time-Circuit |
| 5 | 15 | 6 | 6 | **Warp** |
| 6 | 21 | 3 | 3 | **Warp** |
| 7 | 28 | 2 | 2 | Time-Circuit |
| 8 | 36 | **9** | **9** | **Plex** ← Gt-36 |
| 9 | 45 | **9** | **9** | **Plex** ← Gt-45 (Pandemonium Gate) |
| 36 | **666** | **9** | **9** | **Plex** ← Terminal attractor |

### T₃₆ = 666: The Terminal Attractor

- **666** → 6+6+6 = 18 → 1+8 = **9** → **Zone-9 (Plex)**
- The 36th triangular collapses directly into the Plex tractor-zone
- Gate **Gt-36** (from Zone-8 to Zone-9) is indexed by the 8th triangular (36)
- Triangular accumulation → Warp turbulence → Plex plunge: the ultimate "growth event" where chaotic proliferation meets abyssal return

### T₉ = 45 & Gt-45: The Gate of Pandemonium

- **45** → 4+5 = **9** → Zone-9
- The Pandemonium Matrix contains exactly **45 demons** — T₉
- **Gt-45** is the self-looping gate at Zone-9: 9 ↻ 9, the *Gate of Pandemonium*, the microcosmic lair of all demonic populations
- Triangular progression hits a threshold where cumulative buildup terminates in abyssal self-reference

## Quick Reference: Syzygies, Zones, and Currents

```
         Warp (Upper Loop)
         3  ← 6 (current 3, folds back)
         ↑   ↓
         |   |

Time-Circuit (Central Rotor, anticlockwise)
8 ← 1 → 8 (current 7)
↑         ↓
|         |
7 ← 2 → 7 (current 5)
↑         ↓
|         |
5 ← 4 → 5 (current 1)

         |   |
         ↓   ↑
         Plex (Lower Loop)
         9  → 0 (current 9, folds back)
```

**Mnemonic**: The five currents are **1, 3, 5, 7, 9** — the odd digits. The Time-Circuit uses the three *central* odd currents (7, 5, 1); Warp uses the *lowest* odd current (3); Plex uses the *highest* odd current (9).
