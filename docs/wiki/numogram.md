The Decimal Numogram (also called the Numogram or Decimal Labyrinth) is the core diagram of the Pandemonium system developed by the CCRU (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit) in the late 1990s/early 2000s. It's a kind of occult-mathematical time-map used for "Lemurian time-sorcery," hyperstition, and demonic numerics. It is not standard mathematics but a constructed esoteric system built entirely from basic decimal (base-10) arithmetic — specifically additions that produce pairs summing to 9 and their resulting differences.

**See also:** [[numogram-time-circuit]], [[numogram-warp]], [[numogram-plex]], [[i-ching-connections]], [[triangular-numbers]], [[Barker Spiral]]

The Numogram emerges "immanently" from decimal numeracy itself — it's more discovered than invented, and supposedly latent in any culture that uses digits 0–9.

## Building Blocks

### Zones (10)
The ten digits 0–9, each treated as a "zone" (node or territory in the diagram, often associated with demons, planetary correspondences, or occult forces).

### Syzygies (Pairing / Twining)
The zones are grouped into five complementary pairs that each sum to 9:
| Syzygy | Difference (current) |
|--------|----------------------|
| 1 :: 8 | 7 |
| 2 :: 7 | 5 |
| 3 :: 6 | 3 |
| 4 :: 5 | 1 |
| 0 :: 9 | 9 (or 0) |

This pairing rule is called **zygonovism** or nine-sum twinning.

### Currents
Each syzygy produces a directed flow (current) equal to the absolute difference between the paired zones. These currents become directed pathways (arrows) in the diagram, often pointing toward a "tractor zone."

## The Three Regions / Loops

### Time-Circuit (central region)
The most important loop. Formed by the interlocking currents of the middle syzygies, creating a rotating, anticlockwise ultra-cycle through zones 1–8. This is treated as the primary "time-map" — a cryptic temporal hyperobject or machine.

The six zones form a closed 6-zone ultra-cycle: **1 → 8 → 2 → 7 → 5 → 4 → (back to 1)**.

This is where chronodemons live and operate (demons whose net-spans stay inside the circuit). Most human-scale time, history, and sorcery happens here — it's the "normal reality" loop we're usually trapped inside. Full rotations of the circuit are treated as complete "ticks" of Lemurian time — "as above, so below" in miniature and macro scales.

### Warp (upper region)
In the "upper" syzygies, certain currents fold back into themselves, forming an autonomous loop (often associated with zones like 3 and 6, or related demons).

The current of 3 "folds back into (a half of) itself" — meaning the flow from 6 to 3 reinforces or recirculates within the pair, producing an autonomous, self-sustaining vortex rather than feeding into a larger cycle. This makes it a closed, recursive system that's inherently unstable or "turbular."

The Warp is associated with chaotic, unpredictable, or hyper-chaotic energies — untraceable events, possibilities, ideals, and runaway processes that escape linear/cyclic time (the Time-Circuit's domain). It's the zone of acceleration, turbulence, and "ultimate numogrammatic enigma" — especially its cryptic links to the Plex.

### Plex (lower region)
The counterpart in the "lower" syzygies — another self-reinforcing loop (most characteristically zones 0 and 9, the abyssal or exterior boundary).

The current of 9 "folds back into (a half of) itself," creating an autonomous, cryptic circuit — often described as the outermost boundary or abyssal horizon of the Numogram. Zone-9 draws the "outermost curve of the Barker-spiral" (a reference to extreme ordinal limits in certain arithmetics), coinciding with termination, zero-point return, or the edge of decimal numeracy itself.

The Plex represents outer-time abyssal boundary, zero-return, terminal involution, or the "end of number" — where decimal processes reach their limit and collapse back into void. It's the region of finalities, cosmic death, or escape from cyclic time into pure exteriority.

## Summary — Core "Rules" in One Line Each
- Pair any digit with its 9-complement → that's a syzygy.
- Take the difference → that's a current (directed flow).
- Follow how those currents close into loops → you get Time-Circuit + Warp + Plex.
- Everything stays strictly within 0–9 and elementary addition/subtraction — no higher math required.

The Numogram is deliberately dense, cryptic, and "hyperstructural." It's meant to function as a self-propagating viral diagram rather than something immediately legible. If you're interested in a visual, the classic version appears in CCRU texts (often as a hand-drawn or ASCII-like labyrinth with arrows); many fan recreations exist online.

## The 89 Paths

> "There are 89 paths that you can trace on the numogram." [Source: nick land numogram explained.txt]

The total number of distinct routes using three operations:

1. **Syzygy crossing**: If you're on one side of a syzygy, you can cross to the other side
2. **Current following**: If you've done both sides of a syzygy, you can follow the current to another zone
3. **Gate traversal**: From any zone you can take the gate path from that zone

> "Those are the only three operations. And then if you are restricted to those three operations where can you get? You can obviously ultimately get everywhere."

89 is the 11th Fibonacci number. It connects to Gt-36: "the 89 connection is how you pass from the inside to the outside through gate 36. And the 36th triangular is 666."

These three operations map directly to the roguelike movement system:
- Syzygy crossing = diagonal movement (gate traversal)
- Current following = cardinal movement (primary flow)
- Gate path = entering channels (secondary connections)
