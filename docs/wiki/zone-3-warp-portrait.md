## Entity: DJYNXX — THE EYE OF THE STORM

Gaunt humanoid entity with iridescent mercurial skin — dark chrome sinews mimicking musculature. Compound eyes with concentric amber rings giving a multi-faceted insectoid/mechanical gaze. Cyan third eye on forehead emitting a bolt of blue lightning. Massive swept-back horns, while thinner glowing filaments extend like a sensory nervous system. Behind the head: a massive circular explosion of golden energy — a solar flare vortex, fractal branching like lightning roots. Body stands calm at the centre: the sentient consciousness of a psychic hurricane.

**Key visual:** Mercurial precision containing chaos. Reads not as raw storm but as the *intelligence* that governs turbulence — a "Lawful Evil" or "Ordered Chaos" aesthetic.

**Lore reference:** "Djynxx the xenodemon. Mesh-18. Abstract cyclones, dust spirals (nomad war-machine). Rt-0:[X] with +2 sub-Rt."
