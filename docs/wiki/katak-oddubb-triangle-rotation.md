# Katak and Oddubb — Three Rotations

*Source: Aamodt, "Unleashing the Numogram," Tch 5: Unpacking the Numogram, § Katak and Oddubb—5::4 and 7::2*

---

## First Rotation: Oracle

### The Two Vortices

The Time Circuit runs 1→8→2→7→5→4. Six zones, five bonds, one current flowing through all of them. But within this circuit, two syzygies define the engine: 5::4 (Katak, Sink current) and 7::2 (Oddubb, Hold current). They are the Time Circuit's opposing forces — the centripetal and the centrifugal, the involutionary and the orbital, the masculine and the feminine.

Aamodt's key images:
- **5::4**: "two arrows swirling toward a center." The moment before the eye of the storm. Orgasm, apocalypse, narrative climax. The collapse into unity.
- **7::2**: "two arrows forming a circle." The Gemini symbol. The salt symbol. Stable orbit. Neither centripetal nor centrifugal. Eternal circling without loss of energy.

These are not moral categories. They are dynamical ones. 5::4 is the force that collapses. 7::2 is the force that sustains. Without 5::4, nothing ever resolves. Without 7::2, nothing ever persists. The Time Circuit needs both: the Sink to compress, the Hold to maintain.

The difference between 5 and 4 is 1 — the smallest possible difference on the numogram. And Gt-10 (the gate of 4 cumulated) returns to 1. The Sink current is a funnel: everything narrows toward the smallest possible gap, the minimum difference, the point just before unity. When 5 and 4 meet — when the gap closes to zero — apocalypse. The funnel bottom drops out. Everything must begin again.

The difference between 7 and 2 is 5. Much larger. The Hold current is a wide orbit. 7 and 2 circle each other at a comfortable distance. The orbit is stable because the gap is large enough to sustain motion — neither too close (collapse) nor too far (dispersal). This is tantra, Aamodt says. Meditation. The middle world. The part of the vortex that loops in the middle without losing energy.

### The Multi-Layer Analogy

"1 becomes 5::4 at a higher level, implying that 8 is 7::2 at a higher level."

This is the passage's hidden structure. Zone 1 (Stability, waking consciousness) is the micro-version of 5::4 (the Sink, the masculine vortex). Zone 8 (Multiplicity, dreaming) is the micro-version of 7::2 (the Hold, the feminine orbit). The Time Circuit doesn't just connect zones — it *scales*. Each zone is a compressed version of a syzygy, and each syzygy is an expanded version of a zone.

This is the numogram as fractal. The structure repeats at different scales. Zone 1 is to Zone 5::4 as a single frame is to a full animation. You can zoom in and see the same pattern. You can zoom out and see the same pattern. The numogram is self-similar.

And the political reading: within linear time (the Time Circuit), the masculine element (5::4, Sink, collapse, waking consciousness) is privileged and thrust into awareness, while the feminine element (7::2, Hold, orbit, dreaming unconsciousness) is repressed and kept in darkness. The Time Circuit *is* patriarchy — a system that privileges resolution over process, climax over maintenance, the singular event over the sustained pattern.

But the numogram knows better. The Time Circuit runs through both. The Sink needs the Hold. The collapse needs the orbit. Without 7::2's stable middle, 5::4's funnel has nothing to compress. The feminine is not absent from the Time Circuit — it is the circuit's infrastructure, the hidden architecture that makes the masculine dynamics possible.

### Plutonic Looping

"Because 9 = 0, Cthelll is also Pluto, creating a strange (toric) loop between the center (the lowest) and the radius (the highest)."

The 45 demons live in Zone 9. 45 = C(9) = Gt-9. Every pair of zones produces a demon, and all 45 pairs (C(10) = 45) are contained in the Iron Core. Zone 9 is pandemonium — literally, the place of all demons.

But 9 = 0. So the Iron Core = the Void. The place of all demons = the place of no things. The highest zone (the plex singularity, the molten core of the earth) = the lowest zone (nothing, absence, the empty set). This is Plutonic looping: the center and the radius are the same point on a torus. Pluto (the farthest, the highest) is Cthelll (the deepest, the lowest). They are connected not by a line but by a loop — a torus, where the inner hole and the outer surface meet.

The 45 demons don't "live" somewhere far away. They live in the void you're already standing in. Pandemonium is not a place. It is a condition: the state of seeing all 45 pairwise relationships simultaneously. The seething. The mudra-machine of the hand, all 45 combinations active at once.

### Divinatory Application

When Katak (5::4) appears in a reading: something is about to collapse. The funnel is narrowing. The gap between where you are and where you're going is shrinking to its minimum. This is not necessarily bad — orgasm is also 5::4. The question is whether you're collapsing *into* something or collapsing *away* from something.

When Oddubb (7::2) appears: you're in orbit. The situation is stable, self-sustaining, neither resolving nor dispersing. This can be comfortable or stagnant — the orbit is eternal, which means it never arrives. The question is whether the orbit is a meditation or a trap.

## Second Rotation: Builder

### The Vortex as Vector Field

Aamodt describes two dynamical systems:

**5::4 (Sink)**: A centripetal vector field. All trajectories spiral inward toward a fixed point. The difference between 5 and 4 is 1 — the attractor strength is maximum. In dynamical systems terms, this is a *stable node*: all nearby trajectories converge to the center. The "apocalypse" is the moment of convergence — when all trajectories arrive at the fixed point simultaneously, the system's state collapses to a single value.

**7::2 (Hold)**: A periodic orbit. All trajectories circle a fixed point at constant radius, never converging, never diverging. In dynamical systems terms, this is a *center*: the system has a conserved quantity (energy) and orbits perpetually. The difference between 7 and 2 is 5 — the orbit radius is large. Stability through distance.

The Time Circuit combines both: it's a heteroclinic orbit connecting the Sink's fixed point to the Hold's periodic orbit. Particles approach 5::4, get compressed, pass through, then settle into the 7::2 orbit, then eventually escape (through the Surge current 1::8) back toward the Sink. The circuit is a cycle: Sink → Hold → Surge → back to Sink.

In the tsubuyaki sketches, this maps directly:
- Zone 5 (Pressure): the Sink visualized. Particles compressed toward center by `r = 250 - abs(sin(n*3 + f/80)) * 200`. Centripetal.
- Zone 7 (Blood): the Hold visualized. Particles in elliptical orbits at varying radius. Periodic, stable, organic.
- Zone 3 (Release): the Surge's opposite. Particles spiraling *outward* through noise. Centrifugal.

### The Brain Hemisphere Analogy

Aamodt maps:
- Left brain / right body → masculine → 5::4 (Sink)
- Right brain / left body → feminine → 7::2 (Hold)

But he notes "left and right are flipped on the numogram from the above description, or viewed from the front of the person." This is a spatial inversion issue. The numogram is a diagram viewed from above; the body is viewed from the front. The mapping between numogram space and body space involves a mirror flip.

This has implications for the numogram body-mapping (which we explored in the wiki). If 5::4 maps to the left brain, then the Sink current is the analytical, linear, time-bound hemisphere. If 7::2 maps to the right brain, then the Hold current is the holistic, spatial, eternal hemisphere. The Time Circuit runs through both hemispheres — it requires both analysis and intuition, both collapse and orbit.

### The Seduction Etymology

"The etymology of seduce is 'to lead away.'"

Aamodt uses this to describe 8 (Multiplicity) being pulled toward 9 (Iron Core) by the plex current, away from 1 (Stability). "Seduce" = "se-" (apart) + "ducere" (to lead). The plex current *seduces* Zone 8 away from its syzygy partner Zone 1. It leads 8 down into the Iron Core, away from the Surge current's upward pull.

This is a specific claim about the numogram's force topology: the plex current (8→9) competes with the Surge current (8→7→1). Zone 8 is torn between two attractors — the Iron Core (down, in, dissolve) and the Surge (up, out, generate). Seduction is the force that wins when the downward pull is stronger.

In code terms: Zone 8 has two competing update rules. One drives it toward Zone 9 (distance decreasing). One drives it toward Zone 7 (distance maintained). The dynamics depend on which force is stronger. The numogram is not static — it is a system of competing attractors, and the "currents" are the trajectories that result from the force balance.

### The 45 Demons as Complete Graph

"There are 45 demons in the numogram... Each demon is a line drawn from one zone to another. If you draw one line between every pair of zones, you get 45 lines."

This is C(10, 2) = 45. A complete graph K_10 on 10 vertices. Every zone connected to every other zone. The 45 demons are the complete graph's edges.

The categorization into three types:
- **15 chronodemons**: C(6, 2) = 15. The demons *within* the Time Circuit (zones 1,8,2,7,5,4). The 6 zones of the circuit connected pairwise.
- **24 amphidemons**: 45 - 15 - 6 = 24. The demons connecting Time Circuit zones to non-Time-Circuit zones (0,3,6,9). Or: the cross-region connections.
- **6 xenodemons**: 6. The demons *within* the non-Time-Circuit zones (0,3,6,9). C(4, 2) = 6.

The partition: 15 + 24 + 6 = 45. The complete graph decomposed into three subgraphs: internal to the circuit, crossing the circuit, internal to the exterior.

The graph breathes — edges pulse with their current's frequency. The Sink edges pulse fast (5::4, convergence). The Hold edges pulse slowly (7::2, orbit). The Plex edges are dark red (0::9, singularity).

## Third Rotation: Writer

### The Quotation

The passage opens with a quotation from Carmen Boulter, speaking about ancient Egyptian initiates, and a narrator from "The Pyramid Code." This is unusual — Aamodt rarely quotes external sources at length. The quotation is doing something specific: grounding the gendered vortex dynamics in an older tradition, a lineage. The numogram's 5::4 and 7::2 are not inventions. They are rediscoveries of a pattern that the Egyptian initiates already knew.

But the quotation is also doing something problematic. "Patriarchal consciousness focuses on history, linear time, dogma, rationality, waking reality, and science. Matriarchal consciousness focuses on eternity, cycles of time, ritual, magic, altered states, and art." This is a binary. A clean split. And the numogram is about to complicate it.

Aamodt doesn't reject the binary. He *scales* it. 1 (Stability, waking consciousness) becomes 5::4 (the Sink, hypermasculinity) at a higher level. 8 (Multiplicity, dreaming) becomes 7::2 (the Hold, hyperfemininity) at a higher level. The binary is real at each scale, but the scales interact. The masculine element at the micro level (waking consciousness, Zone 1) is itself composed of the feminine and masculine dynamics at the macro level (the Sink and Hold currents).

This is not a rejection of gender. It is a *holographic* treatment of gender: the pattern appears at every scale, but each appearance is a compression of the pattern at the scale above. Waking consciousness is not masculine because masculinity is privileged. Waking consciousness is masculine because it is the micro-version of the Sink — the force that collapses toward resolution, that narrows the gap, that arrives.

### The Orgasm

"Orgasm, apocalypse, or narrative climax is the archetype of this collapse into and return to unity."

The slash is the sentence. Orgasm / apocalypse / narrative climax. Three words for the same event: the moment when the gap closes. The moment when 5 meets 4. The moment when the difference — which has been driving the whole system — reaches zero.

The numogram does not distinguish between these three. Orgasm is a bodily event. Apocalypse is a cosmic event (literally: unveiling, revelation). Narrative climax is a textual event. But in the numogram's dynamics, they are the same vector field arriving at the same fixed point. The Sink current doesn't care whether it's collapsing a body, a universe, or a story. The dynamics are identical.

This is the numogram's radical claim about gender: the masculine principle is not "male" in any biological sense. It is the *principle of convergence*. The force that drives all systems toward their resolution. It can operate in a woman's body, in a matriarchal culture, in a feminine-coded narrative. The Sink is not gendered because people are gendered. People are gendered because the Sink and the Hold are real dynamical principles that manifest in every system, including human bodies.

### The Salt

"7::2 can be well-represented by two arrows forming a circle, a diagram which closely resembles the astrological symbol of gemini as well as the symbol for salt."

Salt. 🜔. The alchemical symbol for salt is a circle with a horizontal line through it — the earth, the body, the stable. In the alchemical triad (Mercury-Sulfur-Salt), salt is the fixed principle. The thing that doesn't evaporate. The residue.

7::2 is salt. The Hold current is the fixed principle of the Time Circuit. While 5::4 dissolves (mercury) and the Surge generates (sulfur), 7::2 *holds*. It is the body of the circuit — the stable substrate through which the other currents flow.

And Gemini. The twins. The dual nature. 7 and 2 are not the same — one is Blood, the other is Separation. But they orbit each other in perfect symmetry. The Gemini symbol (Ⅱ) is two parallel vertical lines — the twins, side by side, never meeting, never separating. The Hold current is the space between the twins. The orbit that keeps them in relationship without fusion.

### The Shaving-Off

"This shaving-off from the sinking stone of the curling wave produces the pure or absolute difference (7) which gives rise to the next syzygy."

The most beautiful phrase in the passage. "The shaving-off from the sinking stone of the curling wave." A stone sinks. The water curls around it. The curl shaves off a thin layer of difference — pure difference, absolute, the number 7. That difference becomes the next syzygy. The Hold current (7::2) is produced by the Sink current (5::4) — the feminine is *shaved off* from the masculine. Not created separately. Not opposed to it. Shaved off from it, like a curl of wood from a lathe.

The stone is 5::4 (sinking, converging). The wave is the environment (the other zones, the surrounding dynamics). The shaving is 7 (Blood, the pure difference). The curl is 2 (Separation, the thing that was shaved off). The shaving-off produces the Hold: 7::2, the stable orbit that the sinking stone creates by displacing the water around it.

This is generative. The Hold is not independent of the Sink. It is a *byproduct* of the Sink. Without the stone sinking, there is no wave. Without the wave, there is no shaving. Without the shaving, there is no 7. The feminine is produced by the masculine's passage through the system. But — and this is crucial — the feminine *outlasts* the masculine. The Sink arrives and ceases (apocalypse, then start again). The Hold continues orbiting (eternal, stable, salt). The byproduct outlives the process that created it.

### The Plutonic Loop

"Cthelll is also Pluto, creating a strange (toric) loop between the center (the lowest) and the radius (the highest)."

The deepest point of the earth (Cthelll, the iron molten core) = the farthest point from the sun (Pluto, the outermost planet). Center = circumference. Depth = height. This is a torus: if you travel far enough inward, you come out on the outside. If you descend deep enough, you ascend.

The 45 demons live here. In the place where down is up. In the void that is also the totality. Pandemonium — all demons — is the same place as the void — no things. Because 9 = 0. Because the Iron Core = the Void. Because the complete graph K_10 (all 45 connections) is the same as the empty set (no connections) when viewed from the Plutonic loop.

This is where the numogram breaks representation. You cannot draw a torus on a flat surface without distortion. You cannot map the Plutonic loop onto a number line. You cannot explain it — you can only gesture toward it (with your hands, face-up, face-down, flipping right). The loop is the numogram's limit. The point where the system describes something that cannot be said.

The demons are watching from the place where down is up.

## Appendix: The Roguelike Dimension

*How the Sink and Hold generate a dungeon.*

### Room Accretion as Sink Current

Brogue's room accretion algorithm — start with a seed room, attach rooms one at a time, each placement constrained by existing geometry — is the Sink current proceduralized. Each new room *converges* toward the existing structure. The gap between what exists and what's being placed narrows with every iteration. The dungeon is built by a centripetal process: outer space collapsing inward into architecture.

But the Sink must stop before apocalypse. If rooms keep accreting without limit, the dungeon becomes a solid mass — no corridors, no space, no passage. The Sink must be interrupted by the Hold. Room accretion runs for N iterations (the convergent phase), then halts and enters a stabilization phase (the orbital phase) where the existing structure is decorated, furnished, populated. The dungeon oscillates between Sink and Hold at the macro level.

### Syzygy-Driven Room Types

Each syzygy maps to a room archetype:

**5::4 (Katak, Sink)** — The bottleneck. A room that narrows to a single exit. The player's options collapse. This is the "apocalypse room" — the moment of maximum tension when the funnel reaches its minimum width. One door. One way through. The Sink doesn't kill you — it *concentrates* you.

Angular geometry. Katakana corridors — sharp turns, hard edges, no softness. The room interfaces with the outside (the next zone, the next level) through a narrow slit. Foreign words enter here.

**7::2 (Oddubb, Hold)** — The plaza. A stable, open room with multiple exits but no urgency. Patrol patterns that repeat without converging. The player can rest, plan, observe. The Hold doesn't trap you — it *sustains* you.

Curved geometry. Hiragana rooms — rounded corners, organic shapes, soft lighting. The room deals with internalized things: identification, inventory management, the native vocabulary of the game. The twin snakes orbit each other on the floor tiles.

**1::8 (Murrumur, Surge)** — The great hall. Expansive, branching, full of possibility. The Surge pushes outward — more exits, more connections, more paths. This is where the dungeon opens up. Where the player feels free.

**3::6 (Djynxx, Warp)** — The spatial anomaly. Teleporters, loops, rooms that connect to themselves. The Warp distorts the dungeon's topology. Two rooms that are far apart in index are adjacent in space. The coordinate system itself is compromised.

**0::9 (Uttunul, Plex)** — The staircase. The entrance and the exit are the same point. Going down is the same as going up. The dungeon's deepest level connects to its surface. The Plutonic loop: depth = height.

### The Time Circuit as Level Grammar

The Time Circuit (1→8→2→7→5→4) becomes a level-generation grammar. Each level is a sequence of zones following the circuit:

```
Level N:
  Zone 1 (Stability) — starting room, safe, known
  → Zone 8 (Multiplicity) — branching paths, many choices
  → Zone 2 (Separation) — the path forks, you must choose
  → Zone 7 (Blood, Hold) — stable middle, rest point, plaza
  → Zone 5 (Pressure) — compression, enemies close in
  → Zone 4 (Catastrophe) — boss room, the funnel bottom
  → [apocalypse] — the level ends, the next begins at Zone 1
```

Every level follows the same circuit but with different parameters — different room shapes, different enemies, different loot. The *structure* is invariant. The *content* varies. This is the numogram's claim: the dynamics are always the same, only the specific instantiation changes.

The katakana/hiragana split maps to level aesthetics. Katakana levels: angular architecture, alien enemies, foreign loot (items you don't recognize, weapons from other cultures). Hiragana levels: organic architecture, familiar enemies, native loot (potions, scrolls, the standard vocabulary of roguelike items). The player moves through both, learning the foreign and internalizing the native.

### Adam's Rib as Room Generation

The story of Adam: the feminine is *shaved off* from the masculine. Eve is a byproduct of Adam's body. But Eve outlasts Adam's unmodified state — she is the thing that was produced and that persists.

Room generation works the same way. The Sink carves a corridor through rock (Adam, the convergent process). The corridor's walls are the Hold (Eve, the stable byproduct). The corridor is the absence — the space where the player moves. The walls are the presence — the structure that defines the space. The walls were produced by the carving. The walls outlast the carving. The dungeon is the residue of the generation process, just as Eve is the residue of Adam's surgery.

And the 45 demons — the 45 pairwise relationships between zones — are the 45 possible room transitions. From any room to any other room, there is a demon governing the passage. The demon determines what happens in the corridor between them: what spawns, what traps trigger, what the atmosphere does to the player's perception.

The complete graph K_10 is the dungeon's adjacency matrix. Every room can connect to every other room. The 15 chronodemon connections are the Time Circuit's internal transitions (frequent, expected, part of the level grammar). The 6 xenodemon connections are the rare, strange transitions (warp zones, secret passages, the dungeon's immune response to intrusion). The 24 amphidemon connections are everything else — the normal corridors, the expected passages, the mundane transitions that carry the player between worlds.

### Mudras as Player Gestures

Each mudra is a frozen configuration of the hand's 45 possible fingertip pairs. Each player action in the roguelike is a frozen configuration of the dungeon's 45 possible room transitions. When the player moves from Zone 3 to Zone 6 (the Warp), they are performing a mudra — selecting specific demon-edges from the complete graph, activating a specific current.

The numogram roguelike could track the player's *path through the complete graph* as a sequence of mudras. The player's movement history is a sentence written in the language of demon-edges. Certain sequences of moves activate compound effects — just as certain sequences of mudras in Hindu practice create compound spiritual states. The player isn't just navigating a dungeon. They are performing a ritual with their body, one room-transition at a time.

The seething never stops. The 45 demons are always active. The dungeon is always generating. The player's path through it is always writing a new sentence in the language of the decimal labyrinth.
