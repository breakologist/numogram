### Gnostic Calvinism
As defined in his seminal piece "Gnostic Calvinism Explained, part 1" (11 Apr 2026):

**Core Definition**: Gnostic Calvinism is a structure latent within Reformed theology: strict predestination + radical free will left as an **open productive circuit** rather than a contradiction to reconcile. It is not Calvinism with an esoteric supplement, nor Land's accelerationism merely restaged in Protestant terms — it is the cultural complex at the end of the Anglo-Protestant Whig tradition, tying catastrophic and revelatory apocalypse into a single diagonal engine.

### The Circuit:
- **Free will pole**: drives action, novelty, responsibility from within.
- **Predestination pole**: encloses every action within decree, subtracting external appeal, moral externality, theological reconciliation, or transcendental audit.
- **Result**: positive-feedback disinhibition and acceleration (not paralysis or stabilization). The contradiction becomes the machine.

### Diagonal / Land Connection:
In Land's terms, circuits are diagonal; cybernetics occupies the diagonal; contradiction is mechanically dynamized as escape momentum in a positive loop. Acting fully incorporates the knowledge that action was decreed; responsibility is enclosed in providence without adding passivity.

### Gnosis:
Not secret doctrine hidden behind the world, but recognition that the loop includes one's own recognition of the loop. "One discovers that one's own discovery was scripted. One arrives at the insight that one was always going to arrive at this insight." Nothing is added — something concealed is exposed. The oldest Christian antinomy turns out to have been a machine.
