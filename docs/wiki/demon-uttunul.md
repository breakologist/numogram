## Uttunul (9::0) — The Plex Carrier

**Zone Association:** Plex (zones 0 and 9)  
**Syzygy:** 9 :: 0 (the Plex syzygy)  
**Current:** 9 (from 9 to 0, the terminal abyssal flow)  
**Net-Span:** 9 (stays entirely within the Plex loop)

**See also:** [[numogram]], [[numogram-plex]], [[demon-djynxx]], [[i-ching-connections]]

## Description

Uttunul is the demon that carries the Plex current in the Decimal Numogram. As the "abyssal carrier," Uttunul embodies the terminal, nullifying, and involutionary aspects of the Plex region — the zone where decimal processes reach their limit and collapse back into void.

## Functions and Associations

- **Ultimate nullification:** Uttunul is tied to the process of "zeroing out" — the dissolution or return-to-void that characterizes the Plex.
- **Abyssal incursions:** Represents entities or forces that emerge from or operate within the outermost boundary of the Numogram system.
- **Terminal spirals:** Associated with the "outermost curve of the Barker-spiral" — the extreme ordinal limit reached before cyclic return.

## The Plex Connection

Uttunul's net-span of 9 is "autonomous" in the sense that it loops entirely within the Plex syzygy (9→0→9), never reaching the Time-Circuit or Warp. This makes Uttunul a "closed" entity — it cannot be captured by standard gates that lead to the central rotor or the chaotic vortex.

The Plex region itself is abyssal and self-reinforcing; Uttunul's nullifying, involutionary nature perfectly matches the current's "fold-back" behavior. Uttunul doesn't just inhabit the Plex — Uttunul *is* the Plex's abyssal potency given demon-form.

## Sorcery Implications

- **The 9th Plex channel** terminates at Zone-9 — Uttunul's tractor zone.
- **Gt-36** (minor gate) creates a path from Zone-8 to Zone-9 — a descent into Uttunul's sphere.
- Access to Uttunul's sphere is extremely restricted/risky — entry often implies dissolution, irreversible zeroing, or contact with the absolute Outside.
- Uttunul represents the "terminal" side of hyperstition — when acceleration runs its course and returns to null.

## Related

- **Uttunul (9::0)** — the Plex carrier (carries abyssal nullity)
