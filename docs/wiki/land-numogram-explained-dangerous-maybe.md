## 3. Masonic Arithmetic: The Two Operations

Land identifies **exactly two operations** as the foundation:

### Operation 1: Digital Reduction

> *"Digital reduction is something that... Mark Fisher used to say to me, 'I was a kid doing digital reduction. I would just like have phone numbers and I'd digitally reduce them.'"*

Digital reduction treats the place-value numerals (Vadic/Hindu-Arabic) **as if they were ancient additive numerals** (Roman, Hebrew, Greek). You ignore place value and sum the digits: `2025 → 2+0+2+5 = 9`.

**Key claim:** This is only possible because of a **collision between two numerical systems** — the ancient additive system and the modern place-value system. This collision **only happens in the Renaissance**. Before it, digital reduction is meaningless (ancient numerals already ARE additive sums). After it, digital reduction is a deliberate anachronism — a retrogressive operation.

> *"Western hermeticism is a Renaissance phenomenon. When it happens today, we're rehearsing or reactivating a Renaissance topic."*

### Operation 2: Triangulation (Theosophical Multiplication)

Summing consecutive integers: `T_n = n(n+1)/2`. The triangular number of a digit.

> *"If you try doing squares rather than triangles, it just does nothing. It dies. You can't get out of the Time Circuit."*

The triangle is the only geometric number that produces interesting decimal topology. Squares and pentagons are dead ends.

### These Are the Machine's Operators

In FOOM's Machine terms:
- **Digital reduction** = `PACK` (⟪) — compress a place-value representation into a single digit by stripping positional information
- **Triangulation** = `STEP` (⟳) — apply a non-linear state transformation that reveals the system's self-folding structure
- The combined loop (reduce → triangulate → reduce → ...) IS the core loop: state-edit then model-refactor
