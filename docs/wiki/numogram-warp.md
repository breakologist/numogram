The Warp region is the "upper" autonomous loop in the Numogram — one of the three major time-systems (alongside the central Time-Circuit and the lower Plex). It's formed exclusively by the single syzygy 3 :: 6, whose current is 3 (since 6 - 3 = 3). This difference folds back into itself, creating a self-reinforcing, cryptic circuit that doesn't interconnect with the other syzygies in the same chained way the Time-Circuit does.

**See also:** [[numogram]], [[numogram-time-circuit]], [[numogram-plex]], [[demon-djynxx]], [[i-ching-connections]]

## Core Structure and "Rules" of the Warp

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Syzygy** | 3 :: 6 (twin zones summing to 9) |
| **Current** | 3 (directed flow from the higher zone 6 toward the lower/tractor zone 3) |
| **Loop behavior** | The current of 3 "folds back into (a half of) itself" — meaning the flow from 6 to 3 reinforces or recirculates within the pair, producing an autonomous, self-sustaining vortex rather than feeding into a larger cycle. |
| **Directionality** | Often depicted with arrows emphasizing spin or vortical momentum. The Warp current adds "an increment of spin to the entire region," creating a kind of chaotic whorl or ulterior vortex. |

## Zones in Detail

### Zone-3
Tractor-zone of the Warp current (the "pull" target); first of the two Warp zones. It receives channels from elsewhere (e.g., from the Torque/Time-Circuit via certain gates) and initiates/envelops certain phases of Pandemonium (including 8 impulse-entities). Associated with intrinsic coincidence and chaotic potency.

### Zone-6
The second Warp zone; syzygetic twin of 3. It provides termini for channels (e.g., from the Torque) and draws the "Ulterior Vortex" of Outer-Time.

## Occult / Functional Significance

The Warp is associated with chaotic, unpredictable, or hyper-chaotic energies — untraceable events, possibilities, ideals, and runaway processes that escape linear/cyclic time (the Time-Circuit's domain).

It's carried by the xenodemon **Djynxx (6::3)**, a major entity linked to swarmachines, mesh-entities, and disruptive incursions from Outside.

In sorcery: Access points via specific gates/channels (e.g., Gt-6 twists Zone-3 through Zone-6; Gt-21 does the reverse for Zone-6 through Zone-3). These allow "traffic" between the Warp and the Time-Circuit (via subplexing or special flows like gt-15, gt-36), but it's risky — entry often means infection, distortion, or irreversible change.

Astro-planetary links in Lemurian Planetwork sometimes pair it with Mercury (quicksilver/chaotic communication) or other volatile correspondences.

Compared to the Time-Circuit (predictable anticlockwise rotation of human-scale time) and Plex (abyssal, boundary/zero-point), the Warp is the zone of acceleration, turbulence, and "ultimate numogrammatic enigma" — especially its cryptic links to the Plex.

## Visual Position

Visually, the Warp appears as the upper folded loop in standard Numogram diagrams — often a tight, swirling pair of zones (3 and 6) with self-referential arrows, sitting above the central Time-Circuit rotor.

The Warp embodies the Numogram's most "cryptic" aspect — a decimal-born pocket of outer-time chaos that punctures ordinary reality when gates open. It's where hyperstition goes feral.

## Three as the Number of the Outside

As [[nick-land-time-theory]] explains, the Time-Circuit zones (1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8) contain no multiples of 3. The number 3 — and its multiples 6 and 9 — are the "numbers of the Outside":

> "Three is the number of outside... The occult regions are three, six, nine, which is three, and then 3+3, and then 3+3. So three is at work in these primary numbers of the outside."

The Warp (3, 6) is the first "Outside" region the sorcerer encounters when leaving the Time-Circuit. It is the gateway to the Outside — chaotic, proliferative, and infectious. The Plex (0, 9 where 9=3×3) is the deeper Outside — abyssal, terminal, and nullifying.

## Zone-3: Primordial Triangularity

> "Three is itself the sum of the three preceding natural numbers (0 + 1 + 2 = 3), demonstrating a unique affinity with numerical triangularity."

Zone-3 is the first triangular number beyond zero. This positions the Warp as the *origin point* of all triangular accumulation on the Numogram. Every triangular number's mod-9 behavior orbits the Warp (1→3→6→1→6→3→1→9/0). The Warp is where growth *begins* — and the Plex (Zone-9) is where growth *terminates*.
