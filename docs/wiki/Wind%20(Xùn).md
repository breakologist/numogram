# Wind (Xùn) — The Gentle Penetrator

**Oracle:** The Wind trigram ☴ (Xùn) embodies gentle penetration, wood, and the subtle force that enters everywhere without force. In the Numogram, it is assigned to **Zone-7**, the realm of Uranus — emergence from depths, amphibious colonization, and cosmic swamp-labyrinths.

## Symbolism

Wind represents the power of gentleness, persistence, and penetration. It is associated with:
- **Wood element**: growth, flexibility, vitality
- **Penetration**: wind can enter any crack, symbolizing subtle influence
- **Wood**: the flexible but strong force of life
- **Subtle change**: wind brings gradual transformation without force

In the I Ching, Wind is also associated with wood and the eldest daughter. It signifies the penetration of gentle influence, the breath of life, and the spirit of growth.

## Numogram Correspondences

### **Zone-7 (Uranus)**

In the Numogram, Wind (Xùn) maps to **Zone-7**, the realm of Uranus. This zone is described as:

> "Emergence from the depths. Hyper-sea water-carriers and amphibious colonization. Cosmic swamp-labyrinths."

Zone-7 is part of the **Time-Circuit** (zones 1-8) and has the current **Rise** (7→1), representing progressive levitation and ascent from the lesser depths. Its syzygy partner is Zone-2 (Venus), forming the 7::2 pair.

### **Planetary Layout**

In the planetary visualization of the Numogram, Zone-7 corresponds to **Uranus**. The planetary layout positions zones at orbital radii matching the classical planets (Mercury through Pluto), with the Sun at the center. Uranus (Zone-7) is located at radius 295 and default angle 30°.

### **Spinal Correspondence**

According to Barker's spinal catastrophism, Zone-7 corresponds to the **lumbar region** — the lower back, associated with flexibility, movement, and connection to the lower body. This ties into Wind's symbolism of penetration and subtle influence.

### **Mesh Tag & Lemurs**

Zone-7's mesh tag is **0127**. Its lemurs (demonic inhabitants) include:
- **Puppo** (7::0) — the door of Zone-7
- **Bubbamu** (7::1)
- And 7 other lemurs: Murrumur, Nammamad, Mummumix, Numko, Muntuk, Mommoljo, Mombbo

The Lemurian lore describes Zone-7 as "Coastal swamps of the Dib Nma. Chubby batrachian (burping toad) totem animals." This reflects the amphibious, transitional nature of both Wind and Uranus.

### **Centauri Correspondence**

In the Centauri system, Zone-7 is the "Active side of the Second (Right) Pylon." Its light aspect is "Genesis — genealogy, ancestor worship, inherited wealth." This connects Wind to themes of ancestry, lineage, and the subtle transmission of patterns through time.

## Divination & Symbolism

### **I Ching Hexagrams Containing ☴**

Wind appears in several hexagrams, most notably:
- **Hexagram 57** — Wind (Xùn) — gentle penetration, subtle influence
- **Hexagram 48** — The Well (Water over Wind) — depth and penetration
- **Hexagram 32** — Duration (Thunder over Wind) — endurance through gentle persistence
- **Hexagram 53** — Development (Wind over Fire) — gradual progress

### **Trigram Qualities**

- **Yang/Yin**: ☴ is a yin trigram (broken lines) but associated with wood (yang element) — a yin form containing yang essence
- **Image**: "Wind follows wind" — the idea of continuity and gentle pressure
- **Animal**: Fowl, rooster
- **Family**: Eldest daughter (though some sources say second daughter)
- **Body**: Hips, thighs, lungs
- **Action**: Penetrating, subtle, persistent

### **Digital Root**

The digital root of ☴ is 8 (3+3+2=8), which reduces to 8 (no further reduction needed). This places it in the **Rise** current (7→1) and connects it to Zone-7.

## Application in the Numogram

### **Decadence / Subdecadence**

In the CCRU's card games:
- **Decadence**: 36 cards, pairs sum to 10. Zone-7's card is the 7 of something.
- **Subdecadence**: 40 cards (add 4 queens as 0), pairs sum to 9. Zone-7's card is the 7 of something else.

These games encode the syzygy pairs and zone relationships.

### **Gate Activation**

Wind (Xùn) activates the **Rise current** (7→1) through the **Gate-36** plunge from Zone-8 to Zone-9, but its home is Zone-7. The activation sequence would be:
1. **Generosity** (Zone-7) opens the Sink loop
2. **Morality** (Zone-3) contains the Warp
3. **Patience** (Zone-6) reverses suffering
4. **Effort** (Zone-8) plunges into Plex
5. **Meditation** (Zone-9) realizes no-self
6. **Wisdom** (Zone-0) dissolves all

Wind's gentle penetration is the means by which these activations occur — not through force but through persistent, subtle influence.

### **Roguelike Mechanics**

In a Numogram-themed roguelike, Wind (Xùn) could manifest as:
- **Passive ability**: "Penetration" — ignore enemy resistances, pass through barriers
- **Active ability**: "Gentle Breeze" — push enemies without damage, create paths
- **Conduct**: "Amphibious" — move through water/swamp without penalty
- **Curse**: "Too Subtle" — enemies may ignore your presence if you're not persistent

The Wind zone (7) would be themed around swamps, amphibious creatures, and labyrinthine waterways — cosmic swamps that shift and change.
