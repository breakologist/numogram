## Description

Djynxx is the xenodemon (extraneous demon) that carries the Warp current in the Decimal Numogram. As a "xenodemon," Djynxx exists outside the standard Pandemonium classification — a liminal entity that operates at the boundary of the known system.

## Functions and Associations

- **Swarmachines and mesh-entities:** Djynxx is linked to proliferative, self-replicating or networked entities that operate as distributed swarms or "meshes" across the Warp region.
- **Cryptic incursions from Outside:** Djynxx facilitates or embodies incursions from "Outside-Time" — disruptions that cannot be fully contained within the Numogram's time-cycles.
- **Pandemoniac multiplicity:** Associated with swarm-like proliferation, where single entities branch or replicate into vast multiplicities.

## The Warp Connection

Djynxx's net-span of 3 is "autonomous" in the sense that it loops entirely within the Warp syzygy (6→3→6), never reaching the Time-Circuit or Plex. This makes Djynxx a "closed" entity — it cannot be captured by standard gates that lead to the central rotor or the abyssal boundary.

The Warp region itself is turbulent and self-reinforcing; Djynxx's chaotic, vortical nature perfectly matches the current's "fold-back" behavior. Djynxx doesn't just inhabit the Warp — Djynxx *is* the Warp's chaotic potency given demon-form.

## Sorcery Implications

- **Gt-6** (the Gate-6 minor path) twists Zone-3 through Zone-6, allowing traffic between the Warp zones — Djynxx's net-span is exactly this path.
- **Gt-21** reverses the flow (Zone-6 through Zone-3).

- Djynxx represents the "feral" side of hyperstition — when accelerated possibilities escape control and proliferate as mesh-entities.
