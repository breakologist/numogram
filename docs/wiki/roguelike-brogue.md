## Overview

Brogue is a minimalist roguelike with beautiful ASCII graphics and a sophisticated dungeon generation system. Its room accretion design principles map directly onto numogram zone topology.

## Key principles
- Room accretion: rooms are added iteratively rather than planned
- Corridor connectivity through A* pathfinding
- Atmosphere through sparse, high-contrast visual design
