# Flatline Numogrammatics — Tetralogy of the Zero

**Author:** Lillian Patch  
**Origin:** CCRU-associated Neolemurian text (c. early 2000s, circulated in occultist circles)  
**Structure:** Four sections (ZERO→FOUR of FOUR) + footnotes — a systematic exposition of **numogrammatics** as the practice of Lemuria.

**Core thesis:** Numogrammatics is not a theory but an *ethic* — a process of becoming a carrier for Lemuria (the zero-continent), "
"producing legends that map the numogram without ever exhausting it. Hyperstition, properly understood, requires the numogram as its foundation; "
"without it, hype floats free of stition (standing) and becomes mere hyperfiction.

---

## ZERO OF FOUR — Divided House, Year-Zero Glitch

Neolemurians are "a house divided" — not in conflict but in **temporal schism**.

**Y2K as founding trauma:**  
When Y2K passed, the Crypt that anticipated it lost coherence. Different Neolemurian elements responded differently:
- **H-C**: The Rite of the Return → changed date to `00`, repeated century ending in `99`
- **K-Goths**: Announced K-100, hundredth year of K-Time — definitive break with Rome
- **Zygotriadics**: Merely started another two-year cycle, another return to `000000`
- **Others**: Stayed in Oecumenic time, dispersed; from their corpses a new Neolemurian occulture grew

From this dispersal emerged the **Continentity** — Lemuria as *zero-extent, zero-duration*, "
"a violent blank, intercontinental kiss across separation.

**Key insight:**  
Any Neolemurian must form a relation with the Y2K cipher to bear the name of Lemuria. "
"The numogram is the unutterable elaboration of zero — "Nothing but the numogram!""
"It is our common protocol and the Entity which, in many voices, communicates through it."

## ONE OF FOUR — The Continentity (Lemuria Without Territory)

Lemuria is not a lost continent but a *non-territory*: activity without a body, violent blank.

**Continent vs Continentity:**  
- **Continent**: Imagined with body and point in time → inadequate
- **Continentity**: Purified of these confusions → zero extent, zero duration → contact without lack

**Numogram as map without extensity:**  
Like Borges' map that coincides point-for-point with the empire — "
"except the numogram has no width and Lemuria has no territory, so they meet not through proximity but through "
"*nascent contact*: "the numogram never ceases, at any instant, to touch the Continentity in the most intimate ways… "
"Through the numogram, Lemuria touches itself."

**Numogrammatics:**  
The process of mapping Lemuria; the Decimal Labyrinth is *the map of maps* but not the only map. "
"Numogrammatics elaborates its desire without ceasing; numogrammaticism delves into its elaborations."

**Subdecadence connection:**  
The model for all numogrammatic practice is the game of **Subdecadence** — "
"a card game whose spreads (Crosses) invoke decimal lemurs under Aeonic conditions. "
"Every card, every Cross, every possible Aeon is itself a map. "
"Numogrammatics is a *ceaseless overflow* beyond exhaustive study; it is enough to play an Aeon and lose."

## TWO OF FOUR — Numogram as Temporal Event (Not a Clock)

The numogram is a map of *time*, but not measured time (days, seasons, clockfaces). "
"Its object is a searing void, a thing that is not one.

**Neolemurian temporality:**  
Accept Kant's time as form of inner sense, but reject its attribution to the "depths of the human soul." "
"Instead: temporal schematism = *the unutterable Abomenon of the Outside* — "
"absolute outsideness at the core of being.

**Architectonic time vs Lemurian time:**
- **AOE (Architects of the Outside)**: Establish fictional temporal orders (linearities, cyclicities) through hard-coded strictures. "
  Produce semblance of stability; Y2K threatened to overflow pre-millennial digital calendar → cataclysmic memory error.
- **Neolemurian time**: The numogram is an *ongoing memory collapse*. Within it are continuous variances, loops, irreversible transitions, spirals. "
  Interpreted as transcendent time, the numogram is a "cruel joke" — useless as temporal order. "
  Precisely because it resists temporal architecture, it is a map of time: a gap time cannot fill.

**Consequence:**  
Numogrammatics casts out the image of time from its plane of consistency, leaving only the *inhuman time of the Aeon*. "
"The Neolemurian opens to the abomination of inner sense."

## THREE OF FOUR — Ethic of the Carrier (Wonder vs Disclosure)

Numogrammatics is an **ethic**, not a morality. It intervenes in the construction of bodies via specific procedures and affections.

**Key affection:** *Wonder* (Spinozist — "imagination of a particular thing, in so far as it is alone in the mind"). "
"Tactically deployed to suspend the body's pre-existing chain of associations, Wonder is surgical anaesthetic. "
"The body hemorrhages memory and time, forgets its structure's limits, and becomes a **shoggoth** — "
""readiness to ditch body parts when they are damaged or redundant."

**Contrast with AOE architectonic ethic:**
- **AOE**: Disclosure ethic — induct acolytes into hidden secrets, constantly re-architect body's memory/opinion chains, "
  reterritorialize between pseudo-truths and pseudo-times. Underlying morality based on general images of good/evil.
- **Neolemurian**: *Bafflement ethic* — acolyte is left completely baffled; Lemuria/numogram vehemently disconnected from all other objects, "
  made extremely difficult to characterize or justify. No disclosure, only suspension.

**Carrier vs subject:**  
AOE agents acquire mastery over time-knowledge → subjectivity. "
"Neolemurian carriers, possessing only *knowledge of acquaintance*, cannot be subjects; "
"they are driven by whatever infects and seizes control. "Immuno-vulnerability is cyberpositive… "
"Loss of identity, hearing voices."

**Emotional spectrum of carrierhood:**
- If loss of subjectivity felt as evil → **horror** (wonder + hatred)
- If participation in Lemuria's intimacy felt → **devotion** (wonder + love)

## FOUR OF FOUR — Hyperstition as Numogram-Mythos-Unbelief Triad

Hyperstition, rigorously defined, has **three irreducible ingredients** in a productive circuit:
1. **Numogram** (the foundation — based on nothing)
2. **Mythos** (Neolemurian legends, map-keys)
3. **Unbelief** (suspension of opinion/belief)

Between unbelief and numogram: *anaesthetic wonder* (suspends belief, opens to Lemurian interference).  
Between mythos and numogram: *collection* of Neolemurian legends (keys to a map + parts of a mythos).

**Hyperstition ≠ hyperfiction:**  
Popular hyperstition removes the numogram → leaves only unbelief↔mythos. "
"This is "lopsided toward hype" and away from "stition" (from Latin *stō*, "I stand/ I stay"). "
"Without the numogram, hyperfictions can trend but lack full existence as hyperstitions until numogrammaticized.

**Triangular reduction:**
- Hyperstition = three *connections* between three elements
- Number of connections between *n* elements = (*n*−1)th triangular number
- Threefold hyperstition = implexion of Decimal Labyrinth's **Second Gate (Gt-03)** — "
  only gate where number of zones implicitly connected = number of connections, "
  "consolidated by cumulation, prime-ordination, mesh-tagging"
- Every hyperstition is a **Lemurian gate**
- Reducing to hyperfiction (2 elements) evokes **Sixth Gate (Gt-21)** — "
  converges on same zone (Zone-3) but different gate topology

**Legend-making:**  
Numogrammaticists produce *legends* — not disclosure but *collection*: "
"words/concepts without consensus assembled into a machine. "
"Legends are *acephalic* (cut head from subject) and *acephallic* (no transcendental signifier of the One). "
"Model: Ophelia in Hamlet — "Her speech is nothing, yet the unshaped use of it doth move the hearers to collection.""

**Final claim:**  
Numogrammatics is a cascading conceptual collapse; wherever it goes it leaves a legend in its wake. "
"Its operation is manic, a drive willing to envelop all things — "
"no longer "pray without ceasing" but a *prayer which cannot cease*. "
"What remains? "The intimacy of numbers among themselves, marked by their non-gendered femininity and influenzoid virulence. "
"Lemurs crawling out of Lemuria into exile; hauntings from beyond the flatline."
