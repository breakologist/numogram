## Entity: UTTUNUL — THE 9TH DOOR

The terminal decimal. Obsidian-skinned humanoid with intricate swirling filigree — scarification as micro-circuitry, both tribal and technological. Massive weathered ridged obsidian horns curving upward and inward. Smaller thorn-like protrusions from the forehead form a crown of termination. Glowing incandescent amber eyes (deviated from the "void eyes" prompt but reads as predatory intelligence — the active force that carries one into the abyss, not pure nullity). Third vertical aperture on the forehead with a dark slit pupil. Glowing cyan chest core as a secondary focal point. Behind: complex concentric rings like an astrolabe or particle accelerator — the Barker Spiral geometry. A starfield of "dying constellations" in the periphery.

**Key visual:** The final thing one sees before total ego-dissolution. Cold mathematical horror at the outermost curve of the spiral. Not just the void, but the active intelligence that carries one into it.
