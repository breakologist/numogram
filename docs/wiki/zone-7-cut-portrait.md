## Entity: THE INFERNAL NOBILITY

The most dramatically revised entity across batches. Batch 1 (seed 7047): model defaulted to primal reptile — wet amphibian predator, red bulbous eyes, needle teeth, bioluminescent teal scales, blood-dripping tongue. Did not match "decadent demon" direction at all.

Batch 2+ (seed 7777/9007): fully resolved to aristocratic demon lord. Pale marble skin with intricate black ritualistic/vein patterns. Massive swept-back gold/chitin horns with segmented plating and red gemstone in forehead. Ornate black velvet and crimson silk with gold filigree embroidery. Tiered amber cabochon necklace. Intelligent calculating amber eyes with a wide knowing cruel grin. High collared pauldrons.

**Key visual:** Not a monster in the wild — a high-ranking lord of a hellish court. Social and intellectual predator. The "cut" applies to his decisions, not his claws.

**Prompt:** "portrait of an aristocratic decadent demon lord, the Cut personified as infernal nobility, elegant refined features, pale marble skin with obsidian veins, ornate black and crimson velvet clothing with gold embroidery, intelligent calculating amber eyes with a knowing cruel smile, jewelry of obsidian and bloodstone, diabolical sophistication, not a bestial predator but a courtly lord of the abyss"
