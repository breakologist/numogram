# Star-Turns Traversal Order

The order in which the 10 zones appear when walking the outer ring of the chord pentagram.

## SVG Evidence

`chord-pentagram-v2.svg` places zone labels on the pentagram rim in this order:

```
0 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 9 → 8 → 7 → 6 → 5 → 0
(Void)(Surge)(Separation)(Release)(Gate) (Plex)(Multiplicity)(Blood)(Abstraction)(Pressure)→Void
```

A full star-pass is 10 steps.

## Derivation

The outer ring in the chord pentagram places the five syzygy pairs at the
star's vertices; the rim order follows the pentagram's geometry first and
outermoves: Z0→Z1→Z2→Z3→Z4 then Z4→Z9→Z8→Z7→Z6→Z5 through inner-valley
completion up to receipt of Z0.
