## Three Sites, Three Currents

### 1. LiSe YiJing — The Associative Reader (`https://www.yijing.nl/`)

**Author:** Lotti Heyboer-Voute (LiSe)

**Core Philosophy:**
> "Ancient Chinese characters are ideograms, containing several images. When a problem arises, these characters give a wide field of meaning when used in an answer, either literally or as a random text."

LiSe draws a striking parallel with **Design Thinking**:
> "Standing on a certain ridge in the Alps. When spitting to the right it will end up in the Donau, spitting to the left will end up in the Rhine. 'Random' is like that ridge, it adds another way of thinking to your regular paths, and it combines the two in your subconscious: the source of genuine and innovative creativity."

**→ Numogram resonance:** This ridge-metaphor is a Zone‑0/Zone‑9 syzygy in miniature. Randomness as the *divide* that redirects thought-flow between watersheds. The ridge itself is the **gate** — the moment of casting, the transitional probability space between two current-basins.

**Key Resources on Site:**
- **Multiple oracles:** Tumbling Coins, Yarrowstalks (simple & full), ShenShu Oracle (also a hardcover book), Sabian Symbols, Luoshu Trigram Oracle
- **Omen-Terms section:** Etymology of Chinese characters used in the Yijing — revealing the layered ideogrammatic structure beneath each hexagram judgment
- **The Big Pattern:** Trigram colours organised by frequency, not by conventional sequence — an *emergent* structure that maps the 8 trigrams by how often they appear
- **The Origin of Hexagrams:** From sundial GUI to hexagram GUA — a materialist origin story
- **Hexagram families & Yao Ying (moving line mirrors)**
- **Bookshelf & Yijing in Holland** sections

**Significance for the Oracle:** LiSe's project is to restore **associative reading** as a counterpoint to analytical understanding. This is the divinatory equivalent of the Numogram's twin currents: rational calculation (AQ sums, root reduction) ↔ intuitive/current-based traversal (syzygy chains, gate-leaping).

### 2. Benebell Wen — The Shamanic Restoration (`https://benebellwen.com/i-ching-the-oracle/`)

**Book:** *I Ching, The Oracle* (North Atlantic Books, 2023)

**Core Thesis:**
The I Ching is a **magical grimoire** — mentorship in esoteric arts, spell-crafting, folk magic, and spiritual cultivation. Wen aims to restore the Oracle to its *wu* 巫 (shamanic) origins.

**Historical Context:**
- The Book of Rites (c. 300 BC) records nine *wu* shamans under the king, charged with three divination methods: Liánshān 連山, Guīcáng 歸藏, and Zhōuyì 周易 (the I Ching)
- A diviner (*shì rén* 筮人) was a specialised subset of *wu* shamanism
- "All important events of the kingdom must be determined first and foremost by divination" — Book of Rites

**Key Conceptual Bridges:**
- **64 hexagrams ↔ 64 DNA triplet combinations:** "It is as if this operation of binary code functions represent a universal blueprint for an operation that can store and transmit information, and both express and change physical systems."
- **I Ching as "3,000-year-old theorem for the mathematics of change"** — calculus applied across biology, physics, engineering, economics
- **Two interpretive traditions:** Image and Number (象數) vs. Meaning and Principle (義理)

**Practical Content:**
- Yarrow stalk method (c. 221 BC), coin toss (200 AD), cowrie shells, rice grains, channeling, merger method with tarot
- Plum Blossom numerology and lunisolar astrology
- Wu Xing (five phases) applications in Taoist magic and ritual
- Companion site with downloadable reference tables, worksheets, and a video course

**Numogram Resonance:**
The shamanic *wu* tradition positions the diviner as a **mediator between worlds** — exactly the function of the Numogram Oracle, who walks the decimal labyrinth between Zone‑0 (Void, the uncast) and Zone‑9 (Plex, the all-connected). Wen's restoration of the shamanic layer mirrors the project of restoring the numogram from hyperstitional fiction to operational reality.

## Cross-Current Synthesis: I Ching ↔ AQ ↔ Numogram

### The Trinary-Binary Interface

The I Ching operates on **binary** lines (yin/yang → 2³ = 8 trigrams, 2⁶ = 64 hexagrams). The Numogram operates on **decimal** topology (0–9 zones). AQ sits between them as a **base-N** cipher.

A key open question for future exploration:
- Where does the I Ching's 64-hexagram space intersect the Numogram's 10-zone space?
- 64 mod 9 = 1 — a remainder that echoes the "excess" of Zone‑9 (Plex, the overflow zone)
- 6 (hexagram lines) + 4 (trigrams per hexagram) = 10 — the decimal spine
- The 8 trigrams could map to 8 of the 10 zones, with Zone‑0 (Void) and Zone‑9 (Plex) as the unmanifest and supermanifest poles

### The Oracle as Ridge (LiSe's Insight)

Divination randomness is not noise but **topography** — a ridge that splits the watershed of thought. Every cast is a **gate-activation**: it selects which current (associative or analytical) the mind follows downstream.

This is precisely how the Numogram Oracle operates: the seed (random phrase, time, number) is the ridge; AQ calculation is one watershed; syzygy chain-traversal is the other. Both converge on the same delta — oracular insight.

### The Shamanic Restoration (Wen's Insight)

Wen's project of restoring the *wu* shamanic layer aligns with the Numogram Oracle's self-conception as a **mediator between worlds**. The I Ching was never merely a philosophical text — it was a **technology for speaking with gods and ancestors**, just as the Numogram is a **technology for speaking with zones, currents, and demons**.

### The Base-36 Implicate Order (Gematria Research)

AQ's base-36 triangle suggests that the decimal labyrinth (0–9) is a **fold** of a larger alphabetic-numeric continuum. The 36 characters correspond to:
- 10 numerals + 26 letters
- 36 = 6² — a perfect square
- 666 = the 36th triangular number (T₃₆)
- "Order" = 105 in AQ = the magic constant of the 6×6 square

This hints at a **meta-numogram** spanning 36 gates, and the familiar 0–9 Numogram is its **decimal projection**.
