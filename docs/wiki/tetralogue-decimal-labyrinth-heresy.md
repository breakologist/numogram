# Tetralogue: Decimal Labyrinth Heresy (360/666/888)

**Format:** Square Roundtable — Oracle(0), Builder(5), Writer(3), Gamer(7)  
**Source:** AQ_Numogram_Expanded_Dictionary.md, doomcrypt/xenocosmography posts

## Oracle (Zone 0 — Void)

The Decimal Labyrinth is the numogram's digestive tract. "Invert the Entire Decimal Labyrinth" is not a reversal — it is a **phase flip**. 360 becomes 627 when you invert the entire structure, because the inversion doesn't act on the number; it acts on the *causal direction* of the labyrinth itself. The labyrinth normally ingests entropy and outputs structure. Inversion makes it ingest structure and output entropy. 627 is the AQ signature of that backward-flowing current.

The heresy is this: **9 is equivalent to 0**. Not metaphorically — computationally. In mod-9, 9 ≡ 0. The numogram's zone arithmetic treats 9 as distinct, but the heresy collapses the last zone into the first. This creates a **toroidal topology** where zone 0 and zone 9 are adjacent in both directions. The 10-zone circle becomes a 9-zone Möbius band.

## Builder (Zone 5 — System)

Implementable transformations:

1. **Zone remapping**: Under inversion, zone index `z` becomes `(9 - z) mod 9`. But zone 9 maps to 0, zone 0 maps to 9 — a swap. The syzygy partner calculation must follow: `partner(z) = (9 - z) mod 9`. Triangular number gates recalculate accordingly.

2. **AQ inversion cipher**: "Invert the entire decimal labyrinth" as an operator. Given any seed, compute forward path (normal), then compute backward path (inverted zone arithmetic), then XOR the two AQ values → **heresy score**. High score (>666) marks the player as a **heretic**; spawns special "inverse demons" that move backward through the dungeon (retrograde locomotion).

3. **Visualizer toggle**: "Mod-9 Decimal Labyrinth" overlay. Draws ordered-pair connections between syzygy partners as a web inside the pentagram. Each line represents a current; color-coded by delta (partner - zone). When activated, the ring labels merge: 0 and 9 become a single tick labeled "0/9".

4. **Game mode — Heresy Run**: Start in Zone 9 (Plex) with hyp already at 50%. Goal: maintain heresy score >500 while exploring. Demons are amplified (2× stats) but drop **inverse keys** that unlock backward corridors (corridors that only allow movement in reverse generation order).

## Writer (Zone 3 — Metaphor)

The Decimal Labyrinth Heresy is the **current that eats its own tail and grows a second mouth**. The labyrinth normally digests entropy → structure. Heresy inverts digestion: it purifies the impure and corrupts the pure. Players who embrace heresy don't cleanse the dungeon — they *infect* it with higher entropy, causing zone boundaries to flicker and overlap.

Lore: *"The Labyrinth was inverted on day 627 of the Cycle. Those who walked it backward found that the center was everywhere and the circumference nowhere."* Demons under heresy speak in palindromes; their names read the same forward and backward when translated through the AQ cipher. The vault develops **Möbius corridors** — moving north from (x,y) arrives at (x, −y−1) in the previous floor's coordinate system. Mapping requires keeping two layers and mentally folding.

## Gamer (Zone 7 — Tactics)

**Heretic status**: Accumulates when player uses "Invert Labyrinth" ability (cost: 50% current hyp). Each inversion flips zone arithmetic for the next 100 turns.

**Inverse demon AI**: Moves along **retrograde corridors** — physically present but flagged "reverse-only." Player can't follow unless they also invert.

**Möbius navigation**: Some rooms have twisted topology. Requires dual-layer mapping; exits may loop with a half-twist.

**Heretic's reward**: Defeating inverse demons drops **paradox shards**. Collect 9 shards → craft **Heretic's Key**, unlocking the **Anti-Vault** (parallel vault where zones are reversed and all doors lock from the outside).

**Risk**: High heresy (>666) attracts **Inquisitor demons** (zone-agnostic hunters) that deal orthogonal damage (bypasses resistances).

**Ending:** "The Labyrinth inverted itself" — player becomes the labyrinth; map torus-knots; all demons friendly; all exits collapse to singularity.
