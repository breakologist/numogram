# Powers of 5

The CCRU "powers of 5" are the backward (anticlockwise) cycle from the
Powers of 2 diagram, traversed opposite to the forward powers-of-2 ring.

## Diagram text (powers-of-2-circular.svg)

```
Powers of 2 (cyan, forward):  1 → 2 → 4 → 8 → 7 → 5 → 1
Powers of 5 (amber, backward):5 → 7 → 8 → 4 → 2 → 1 → 5
     ▲ 1-2-4          ▽ 8-7-5   (two interlocking triangles)
```

Both share the same 6 elements in opposite order — neither is prior.

## Relation to TC ≡ 2²

In [[tc-≡-2²]] the TC-set = zone 4 (centroid at `2² = 4`). Powers-of-5
converge on the same node (4) in the backward direction, confirming that
the TC-set is geometrically管辖 both forward and backward circulations.
