# Twin Serpent Quote (CCRU)

> Two prime factors of 10 run time forever in opposed directions,
> neither obviously favored.
> — Nick Land

The twin serpent diagram in `powers-of-2-circular.svg` represents the
two interlocking circular waveforms:

## Powers of 2 (Clockwise, Cyan)

`1 → 2 → 4 → 8 → 7 → 5 → 1` — expansion (1, 2, 4 as godhead apex)

## Powers of 5 (Anticlockwise, Amber)

`5 → 7 → 8 → 4 → 2 → 1 → 5` — contraction (5, 7, 8 as base triangle)

## "Neither obviously favored"

Both cycles are symmetric after 6 steps. The diagram's punchline:
power and anti-power are not hierarchical — both extend infinitely,
neither dominates by structural asymmetry.
