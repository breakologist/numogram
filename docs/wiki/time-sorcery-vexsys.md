# Time Sorcery — Vexsys / Gate Zero

The practical manual of numogrammatic magic, written by Vexsys (carrier of Gate Zero), published 2021. The most systematic attempt to explain how numogrammatic magic actually works — from fundamentals through qabbalism, lemurian entities, hyperstition, to the Necronomicon. "Dormant since 2023."

[Source: Time Sorcery - Vexsys.epub, Gate Zero, 2021]

## Key Revelations

### The Pandemonium Matrix Has Errors

> "The Pandemonium Matrix, as written, is riddled with errors and inconsistencies... Vysparov's errors are an invitation for us to edit, cut-up, or completely do away with the information he's supplied to us."

This is the most important practical insight: the 45-demon Pandemonium Matrix is NOT canonical. It contains errors inherited from Vysparov's wartime work and Stillwell's reconstruction. Each practitioner must develop their own corrected version.

### The Lemurian Necronomicon Is Incomplete

> "The Pandemonium Matrix as written by the Ccru is labeled 'extracts of the Lemurian Necronomicon.' That means that there's still a book to be found."

The Necronomicon is not a finished text — it is an ongoing project. "The great work of the individual time-sorcerer is to update their Necronomicon as they receive new information or find a weak link in their qabbala. This turns the Necronomicon into a book of the living dead."

### Ascryption: The Naming Rite

> "The secret sauce of hyperstition is found in ascryption, defined as the occult process by which something receives a name."

> "My rituals for ascryption generally involve massive amounts of gematria and glossolalia until I stumble upon the perfect name by intuition."

The process: generate noise (glossolalia), compute AQ values (gematria), wait for the name that "clicks." Not synthetic qabbala (deliberately targeting a value) but intuitive discovery.

### Against Synthetic Qabbala

> "If you try too hard to hit upon a certain AQ value it does seem that you miss out on some levels of hype, as if the lemurs punish you for your vigorously mathematico-logical nature."

> "It seems best to be as fast and loose as possible, focusing on intuitive approaches instead of logical ones."

This echoes [[nick-land-time-theory|Land's]] "innocence paradox": the signal is strongest when it wasn't deliberately planted. Synthetic qabbala works (the audience may not tell the difference) but loses the "lemur blessing" — the hyperstitional charge that comes from genuine discovery.

### Zone 2/7 as Practical Divination

> "Say you've got a date tomorrow and you're trying to come up with what to do. When you're generating the possibilities, that's Zone-2. If you're not actually applying Zone-7 (some kind of metric to determine the most suitable option), you'll find yourself tumbling through Gt-3 into the warp."

This is the most concrete practical example of zone-function in the entire corpus. Zone-2 (Separation, Linkage) = generating possibilities. Zone-7 (Blood, DNA) = applying a metric to select. Gt-3 = the gate from Zone-2 to Zone-3 (Warp) — where you go when you generate possibilities without selecting.

### Dedication to Lurgo

> "This work is dedicated to Lurgo, Terminal Initiator, Door of Doors, Mercurial Amphidemon of Openings."

Lurgo (Mesh-00, Net-Span 1::0) is the first demon — the Amphidemon of Openings. The book begins at the beginning: Zone-1 (First Door), through Lurgo (the Door of Doors).
