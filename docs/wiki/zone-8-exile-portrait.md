## Entity: THE MU NMA ABOMINATION

The most characterful and variable entity across all batches. Batch 1 (seed 8086): feminine gaunt face with bioluminescent pink luminous eyes, jellyfish dome crown, anglerfish lure dangling from mouth, third eye in forehead. Reads as a transhuman exile — survivor of abyssal transformation with personality behind the eyes.

Batch 2 (seed 7778): crown evolved into five distinct floral-medusozoan bells in radial array. Glowing braided root/filament emerging from the mouth, running down the chest. More architectural and regal.

v3 (seed 9008): Most ethereal and ghostly. Translucent brain-like cranium with neural patterns. Twin massive glowing celestial orbs flanking the head. Body appears woven from pure light and neural fibers rather than physical matter. Crown of bioluminescent cyan tentacles and vine-structures. Central energy conduit from chin to glowing chest core.

**Key visual:** Pure energy and consciousness manifesting in the void. "She who survived the crushing depths by becoming one with them." The entity that looks back.

**Prompt:** "portrait of a deep sea entity, the Exile made flesh, Lukh and Shuplu spirit-diffraction visible as bioluminescent tendrils, moonlight-white skin with three-fold blooming petal formations, polytendrilled abomination from the Mu Nma submarine cities, the pleasure-blow, the moan of infinite depth"
