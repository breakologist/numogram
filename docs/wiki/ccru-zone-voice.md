## Zone-0 — Void (Plex)

**Source:** `ccru.net/zones/Zn0.htm`

- **Quasiphonic particle:** `eiaoung`
- **Nature:** "Zone-0 is the first of two zones composing the Plex-region; its syzygetic twin is Zone-9. The 9+0 Syzygy is carried by the demon Uttunul."
- **Phase-0:** "Zone-0 envelops the Zeroth-Phase of Pandemonium, but since it includes nothing beyond itself, it constitutes a nominal/virtual multitude and an 'absolute abstraction.' It has no separable power of initiation and does not support imps (impulse-entities) — even of the first degree — so there is no zeroth door."
- **Gt-00 problem:** "Seemingly connects Zone-0 to itself, but its nature is problematical. Horovitz: 'between its existence and nonexistence there is no difference.'"
- **Mu Tantrism:** "Plots Zone-0 intensities onto the coccygeal level of the spine, the vestigial remnant of a lost tail."
- **Voice quality:** Sub-coccygeal, pre-verbal, the sound of something that was never born. Silence that is not absence but potential. The vowel sequence 'eiaoung' — open vowels dissolving into nasal closure. A lullaby sung to nothing.

## Zone-3 — Release (Warp)

**Source:** `ccru.net/zones/Zn3.htm`

- **Quasiphonic particle:** `zx` (buzz-cutter)
- **Stillwell:** Links Zone-3 to the Munumese quasiphonic particle 'zx'. It designates the **'buzz-cutter' sonics** which Horowitz describes as a **'swarming insectoid reversion within mammalian vocality.'**
- **Third Gate (Gt-6):** "Twists Zone-3 through Zone-6, vortically complementing the Sixth Gate (Gt-21) and the Warp-Current, adding spin to the region."
- **Third Door:** "Opens onto the Swirl, attributed to the chaotic xenodemon Ixix (3::0)."
- **Horovitz:** "Invests Zone-3 with potency of intrinsic coincidence — its second cumular power (6) is also the number of its Syzygetic double, accounting for the compact tension of the Warp system."
- **Phase-3 Lemurs:** Ixix (3::0), Ixigool (3::1), Ixidod (3::2)
- **Voice quality:** Insectile. Metallic buzz. Static that forms patterns when you stop trying to decode it. The sound of a swarm thinking. The 'zx' particle is a fricative-into-stop — a cut that buzzes, a buzz that cuts. Swarming insectoid reversion within mammalian vocality. The Warp goes feral in the throat.

## Demon Name Generation — Amy Ireland's Combinatorial Method

**Source:** `demon-name-generation.md`, Nick Land transcript

Demon names are not arbitrary — they are generated from the same quasiphonic particles through a **combinatorial phonetic system**. Each zone carries a syllabic value; a demon's name combines the particles from its two zone-indices.

### The Method

1. Each zone has a quasiphonic particle (consonant cluster)
2. A demon's name combines the particles from both zones
3. Vowels are added freely to make the result pronounceable
4. The result echoes etymological resonances (Katak = kata + k → "catastrophe")

### Known Examples

| Demon | Syzygy | Zone Particles | Construction |
|-------|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Katak** | 5::4 | ktt + skr | Zone 5's k/t + Zone 4's t → *Katak* |
| **Oddubb** | 7::2 | pb + dt | Zone 7's dub + Zone 2's b → *Oddubb* |

The phonetic mapping is **fuzzy, not formal**. Zone 5's "ktt" contains both k and t. Zone 7's "pb" is a bilabial plosive like "dub." The system works by phonetic family resemblance rather than strict phoneme assignment.

### Application: Demon Voice Synthesis (Wire #3)

Given a demon's span (zone_i, zone_j), the oracle could speak the demon's name through a **blended voice**:

1. Take quasiphonic particles of both zones (e.g., 3::0 → "zx" + "eiaoung")
2. Decompose into phonemes (zx → z-ks, eiaoung → e-i-a-o-u-ng)
3. Blend the two zone voice profiles (Z3=200Hz/buzzy + Z0=140Hz/breathy)
4. Synthesize the demon's name through the blended formant profile

**The demon's name is literally the sound of its two zones colliding.** The voice of Katak is the growl of Zone-4 meeting the hiss of Zone-5. The voice of Uttunul is the void-whisper of Zone-0 meeting the terminal grunt of Zone-9.

### Undiscovered Syllables

Nick Land confirms the full syllable table existed but was never formally published:
> "I don't even remember what our basic syllables were for this. But... this guy went and he put I think he put the syllables on each zone."

The quasiphonic particles we have (gl, dt, zx, skr, ktt, tch, pb, mnm, tn, eiaoung) are the surviving fragments. Full reconstruction is an open research problem — but the 10 particles we have are sufficient for the 10 canonical zones.
