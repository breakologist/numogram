# Decadence (Atlantean Side)

The Lemurian half of the [[barker-spiral]]: the occulted variation whose sum-to-9 twinings reveal the *subdecadent underworld*. In Barker's narrative, Subdecadence was the "casually mentioned" variation that unlocked the crisis.

> \"Subdecadence introduces zeroes, and nine-zero twins. It works by zygonovic numerism.\"
> — Daniel Charles Barker, *Barker Speaks* (CCRU interview, Autumn 1998)

## Rules (Reconstructed)

- **Players:** 2 or more
- **Equipment:** Two standard decks of cards
- **Objective:** Form pairs whose values sum to **10**
- **Scoring:** Zero is sovereign (9 wraps to 0 via 9‐sum complementarity)

Each card maps to a digit (Ace = 1 through 10 = 0). Pairs sum to ten: 0+10, 1+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5. The twist: zero is *not* a void but a *saturated convergence* — since 9 wraps to 0 in decimal reduction, the nine‐sum becomes the zero‐sum.

## Lemurian Numeracy

Subdecadence operates on **9‐sum equivalence**: x + (9 − x) = 9 ≡ 0 (mod 9). This is *zygonovoc numerism* — the art of twin pairing under the non‐decimal (decadence‐minus‐one) regime.

```
        0  (9 via 9+0; zero as saturated null)
        ↓
9 — 9+0=9 (≡0)  0 — 0+9=9 (≡0)
        ↗           ↖
8 — 8+1=9        1 — 1+8=9
        ↗           ↖
7 — 7+2=9        2 — 2+7=9
        ↗           ↖
6 — 6+3=9        3 — 3+6=9
        ↘           ↙
         4⊕5 (sums to nine; the Lemurian axis)
```

The Lemurian axis is a *diagonal mirror* through the digit set. Every number acquires a complement 9 − x. This is the *geotraumatic* register: the crack in decimal coherence where traumatic memory (Cthelll, the iron core) leaks through.

## Geotraumatic Undertow

Where Decadence enforces surface symmetry (10 = return to origin), Subdecadence reveals the **9‐gap**. Nine is the last digit before wrap; it is also the point where digital reduction *fails to resolve*:

> \"Once numbers are no longer overcoded, and thus released from their metric function, they are freed for other things, and tend to become diagrammatic.\"
> 
> \"Treat the decimal numerals as a set of 9‑sum twins — zygonovize — and they map an abstract intensive wave, indifferent to magnitude. Everything efficient about digital reduction is concerned with this, since it discovers the key to decimal syzygetic complementarity: 9 = 0.\"

Subdecadence thus maps the **crypt of the decimal system**. If Decadence is the manifest order (10‐based, capitalist numeracy), Subdecadence is the latent code (9‐based, geotraumatic register). The spiral does not emerge until *both* registers are held simultaneously.

## Gameplay Summary

From the online implementations and CCRU descriptions:

**Objective:** Survive as long as possible without letting the cumulative score drop to zero or below.

**Setup:**
- Two standard 36-card decks (ranks 1–9, four suits each)
- Cards are dealt into two sets:
  - **Set-1 (Atlantean Cross):** Five cards dealt face-up in a cross pattern
  - **Set-2:** Five cards dealt face-down in a row below

**Gameplay:**
- Flip the top card of Set-2 one at a time.
- If it can be paired with a face-up Set-1 card to sum to 10 (e.g., 1+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5), click that Set-1 card to bind them.
- **Bonuses:** Certain pairs yield bonus points:
  - 1 + 9 = +8
  - 2 + 8 = +6
  - 3 + 7 = +4
  - 4 + 6 = +2
  - 5 + 5 = 0 (no bonus)
- **Penalties:** Any unpaired Set-1 card at the end of a round incurs a penalty equal to its rank (e.g., an unpaired 7 costs 7 points).
- **Collapse:** When the cumulative score drops to 0 or below, the aeon fractures and a demon from the Pandemonium Matrix manifests, ending the game.

**Example:** If Set-1 shows 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 and the first three Set-2 cards are 9, 2, and 4:
- Pair 1+9 = +8 (bonus)
- Pair 3+7 = +4 (bonus)
- 5 remains unpaired → -5 penalty
- Cumulative score: 8 + 4 - 5 = +7

## The CCRU and Pandemonium Context

Decadence is more than a card game — it is a **divination system** and **sorcery practice** within the CCRU's Pandemonium framework. It is associated with the Atlantean side of the Barker Spiral, representing the sum-to-10 twinings that produce the decadent surface.

From the CCRU writings:
> \"Pandemonium is the complete system of Lemurian demonism and time sorcery. It consists of two principal components: Numogram (time-map) and Matrix (listing the names, numbers and attributes of the demons).\"

Decadence implements this system through gameplay: the 36-card deck corresponds to the 36 demons of the Pandemonium Matrix (excluding the 9 demons of the Plex and the 9 of the Warp, leaving 18? Actually, the full matrix has 45 demons, but Decadence uses 36 cards — possibly representing the 36 decans or the 36 tiles of the Atlantean Cross). The game's mechanics of pairing to 10 and summoning demons when the score collapses mirror the Numogram's syzygies and the Pandemonium's demonic invocations.

The online implementation at [playdecadence.online](https://www.playdecadence.online/) describes it as:
> \"DECADENCE — CCRU Pandemonium Matrix Card Game & Oracle | Numogram Time-Sorcery\"
> 
> \"Play the CCRU's Atlantean time-sorcery card game online. The most comprehensive digital implementation of the Cybernetic Culture Research Unit's Pandemonium system.\"
