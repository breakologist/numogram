## 2. Explore

I conducted a deep investigation into the spectral centroid discrepancy between MIR and librosa methods. Key findings:

- **Root cause**: MIR uses Welch's method on entire signal, capturing true spectral content of transients
- **Librosa issue**: Frame-based centroid calculation gets diluted by long silences (non-silent ratio 0.22-0.75%)
- **Sample rate mismatch confirmed**: MOD uses 8363 Hz, MIR expects 44.1 kHz, causing artificial lowering of centroids (200-500 Hz vs expected 5000-9000 Hz)

I traced the audio generation pipeline and discovered that zone audio files are generated by the Phase 4 dataset generation process, which creates MOD files and renders them to WAV.

## 3. Reflect
