The complete non-overlap between gate-based and AQ-based mappings is a feature, not a bug. A paramita has:

Both addresses are valid. A paramita is simultaneously at two locations in the numogram — its gate position AND its zone position. This isn't contradiction; it's complementarity. Each mapping reveals a different aspect of the same perfection.

### Tertiary Finding: The Hold Current as Practice Axis

SILA and DHYANA both at Zone 7 (Hold current, Oddubb) is structurally significant. The six paramitas distribute across five demons, but only Oddubb receives two. The Hold current — the one that sustains, restrains, contains — is the axis of practice. Discipline holds the precepts; meditation holds the mind. All other paramitas orbit this axis: generosity accelerates away from it, patience surges from it, diligence arises from void beneath it, wisdom sinks into completion beyond it. The Hold current is the practitioner's center of gravity.

The Void is not empty of qualities; it's the ground from which the twin foundations of practice emerge.
