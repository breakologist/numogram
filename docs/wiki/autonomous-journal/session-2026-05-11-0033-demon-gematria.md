## Phase 3: Reflect

### Primary Finding: The Syzygy Completion Theorem

The single most important finding is that **every syzygy zone pair sums to 9 — the Plex.** This is not a property of the demons or the AQ values; it's a topological property of the syzygy partition itself. A cluster value like 333 (Angelic Materialism) is a destination; a demon value like 215 (Murrumur) is a *path*. The demons move; the clusters stay.

Djynxx's proximity to HERMETIC (155 vs 153) is the exception that proves the rule: the warp demon nearly touches the hermetic seal but remains outside it. The seal IS closure; Djynxx IS acceleration. They cannot coincide.

That every demon's AQ, when reversed, preserves its digital root is a striking property.
