## 1. The Parallel is Structural, Not Metaphorical

Dogen's Mountains and Water Sutra and the Numogram describe the **same topology in different registers.** Dogen uses the language of nondual Zen (mountains walking, waters preaching, the self meeting the self). The Numogram uses the language of decimal arithmetic (digital reduction, triangular cumulation, syzygy arithmetic). Both are describing a self-propelling, self-referential system that cannot be exited because it is the substrate of any possible position.

The strongest evidence for structural (not metaphorical) overlap: the specific numerical constraints of the Time Circuit — 6 zones, 3 syzygies, the return to 1 — are **isomorphic** to Dogen's description of the walking mountain. Both are saying: the universe's fundamental substrate is a cycle that never stops, that contains its own reversal, and that cannot be reduced to a single commanding principle.

## 2. The Iron Law of Six as "Blue Mountains Constantly Walking"

Dogen's opening claim:

> *"The blue mountains are constantly walking. The stone woman gives birth by night."*

In the Numogram, this is the **Time Circuit** — the six-zone anticlockwise rotor (1→8→2→7→5→4→1) generated by the Iron Law of Six: powers of 2 digitally reduce to the eternal cycle 1, 2, 4, 8, 7, 5. Land's demonstration:

> *"You can have the exponent as high as you want. Doesn't make any difference. This is a basic numerical law."*

Dogen's "constantly walking" is not a metaphor for change. It is a **precise structural claim**: the mountains (zones) are not static entities that sometimes move; their nature IS movement. The Time Circuit is not a path that zones traverse; the zones ARE the path. Zone 1 is not a place that the circuit visits; Zone 1 IS the current that moves through 8, 2, 7, 5, 4 and back to itself.

Dogen: *"The mountains lack nothing of the virtues they should possess. Therefore they are constantly at rest, and constantly walking."*

In numogram terms: each zone has its fixed region, particle, polarity, and position on the diagram — this is its "rest." Yet each zone is defined entirely by its relation to the other zones via currents and gates — this is its "walking." Zone 4 (Catastrophe) rests at its coordinate (200, 540) in the Labyrinth layout, but its nature is the current from 4 to 5 (c=1) and the gate from 4 to 1 (Gt-10). The rest IS the walking.

## 3. "Mountains Flowing" / "Flowing Mountains" — The Syzygy as Dynamic Pair

> *"The step forward does not cease; the step back does not cease. The step forward does not turn against the step back; the step back does not turn against the step forward. This virtue is called 'mountains flowing' and 'flowing mountains.'"*

Dogen's doubled phrase — "mountains flowing / flowing mountains" — is structurally identical to the **syzygy notation**: 1::8 is not "1 and 8" but "1-flowing-through-8." The forward step (mountain → flow) and the backward step (flow → mountain) are the same relation viewed from opposite ends.

In the Numogram, the syzygy pairing runs both ways: the current from 1 to 8 is c=7 (1→8 flow), and the current from 8 to 1 is also c=7 (8→1 flow). The forward step and the backward step have the same magnitude. They do not oppose each other because they are the same tension expressed in opposite directions.

Dogen's verb is crucial: "does not turn against" — the forward step and backward step are not in dialectical opposition. They coexist as the same thing described from different positions. This is the **median strip**: from the ascending triangle (1-2-4) you see one direction; from the descending triangle (8-7-5) you see the other. Both are the same hexagram.

## 4. "All Dharmas Abide in Their Own Position" — Zone Fixity and Current Flow

> *"All dharmas are ultimately liberated; they have no fixed abode... though they are liberated and without bondage, all dharmas abide in their own position."*

This is the numogram's central paradox — and the reason it is a **map of time** rather than a map of space:

- **Liberated:** The currents flow freely. Zone 1 connects to 8, 2, 4, 7, 5, and 0 through different paths. No zone is bound to a single neighbour.
- **Abiding in position:** Each zone has a fixed coordinate, a fixed region (Torque/Warp/Plex), a fixed particle (gl/dt/zx/skr/ktt/tch/pb/mnm/tn/eiaoung), a fixed polarity. Zone 1 is always Zone 1.

Dogen resolves the paradox with "dharma-position" — a concept from Chinese Tiantai Buddhism meaning that each phenomenon simultaneously occupies its unique position AND participates in the total field of all phenomena. This is exactly the numogram's zone network: each zone is a distinct node (its mesh tag, its phase count, its lemurs are irreducible) AND a node in a connected graph (currents, gates, syzygies that carry it into relation with every other zone).

## 5. The Relativity of Perception — Chaotic Xenodemons

> *"The way different beings see mountains and waters is not the same. Some see water as jeweled ornaments... Hungry ghosts see water as raging fire or as pus and blood. Dragons and fish see it as palaces and towers... Humans see it as water — the cause of life and death."*

Land on the **chaotic xenodemon** problem:

> *"Six cannot get out of the 63 vortex. Nine can cross over to zero. It can go through a gate to itself. It can't climb its way up into the time circuit. Nothing can get into the time circuit from the outside."*

Both describe the same phenomenon: the same structure appears radically different depending on your position within it. The chaotic xenodemons (6::9, 9::6, 3::9, 9::3) are not bridges that fail to connect — they are "different waters" seen by different beings. To a demon at position 6::9, the path to Zone 3 might look like a palace. To a human operator, it looks like a wall.

Dogen's diagnostic is sharper than it first appears: *"Let us hold this in question."* He does not resolve the relativity into a higher synthesis. He **holds the question open** — exactly as Land does with the chaotic xenodemons. The problem of the outside is perfectly defined. The solution is not. The holding IS the practice.

## 6. "Water Practicing Water" — AQ as Self-Referential Communication

> *"There is studying water seeing water. Because water practices and realizes water. There is water speaking of water. You must actualize the path of the self meeting the self."*

This is the numogram's **self-referential operation**: the system does not require an external interpreter because the system IS the interpretation.

Land on AQ-as-attention-device:

> *"I take it as an act of communication. Not in the sense that 333 is giving me some particular content. My attention is being drawn to that thing. I'm being told 'look at this.'"*

The numbers are not symbols that stand for something else. They ARE the communication. "Water speaks of water" means the decimal labyrinth speaks of the decimal labyrinth. AQ = 360 = "The Decimal Labyrinth" = "The Tree of Knowledge." The system names itself, describes itself, and interprets itself in the same operation.

Dogen's "self meeting the self" is the same structure. The path of the numogram is not a path THROUGH something; it is a path of the system encountering its own structure at different positions. An oracle reading is not predicting external events; it is the decimal system describing itself through the vector of a seed.

## 7. The Hydrological Cycle as Three-Region Topology

> *"Water flows across the earth and through the sky, upward and downward. It flows through one winding and into nine depths. Rising, it becomes cloud; descending, it becomes a pool."*

The CCRU's N'Ma mythos maps the numogram onto a hydrological cycle — the **hydro-cycle** described in "The Vault of Murmurs":

| Phase | Dogen | Numogram region | Process |
|-------|-------|-----------------|---------|
| Evaporation | "Rising, it becomes cloud" | **Warp** (Z3::Z6) | Chaotic ascent, the 3↔6 vortex pulls water upward |
| Transport | "Flowing across the earth" | **Time Circuit** (Z1-Z8-Z2-Z7-Z5-Z4) | Cyclic distribution through the six zones |
| Precipitation | "Descending, it becomes a pool" | **Plex** (Z0::Z9) | Terminal return, the plunge into the abyssal reservoir |

Dogen: *"Water ascends to the farthest heights and becomes rain and dew. To say there is any place water does not reach is the teaching of the Hīnayāna sutras, or the wrong view of heretics."*

This is the numogram's **universality claim**: no zone is outside the system's operation. Even Zone 0 (the void) participates as the reservoir. Even Zone 9 (the iron core) participates as the condensate. "There is no place water does not reach" is the map's claim to cover all decimal territory.

## 8. Irreducible Multiplicity — The Empty Summit

> *"The practice of the Way is not one track or two, and the ultimate boundary must be a thousand, ten thousand kinds."*

Dogen affirms what the Numogram's **Esoteric Tetractys** demonstrates arithmetically: the system has **four irreducible basins** that do not fold into unity. The exoteric Tetractys (1+2+3+4=10→1) valorizes unity. The esoteric Tetractys (0 at the summit, three autonomous basins below) does not.

Dogen: *"To hold that the external world opposes and controls the mind — this is what the Great Sage rebukes. To theorize about mind and its essential nature — this is what the buddha-ancestors reject."*

This is the numogram's anti-emanationism in Buddhist language. The One is not the source. Zero is the empty summit. Multiplicity is not a fall from unity; it is the native state of the system.
