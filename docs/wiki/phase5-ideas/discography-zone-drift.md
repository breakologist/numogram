Hypothesis: artists migrate across the hypercube over their career. Test by:
- Sampling ≥1 track per album for artists with ≥10 albums (Autechre, Earth, Boris, Tim Hecker, Cocteau Twins)
- Plotting zone predictions over release year
- Looking for trends (e.g., "drift toward Zone 1 with age", "early Zone 7 → late Zone 2")
This turns the zone classifier into a *biographical instrument*, reading an artist's esoteric trajectory.
