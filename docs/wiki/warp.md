# Warp

The **Warp** is the upper autonomous loop of the Decimal Numogram — one of the three principal time-systems alongside the central Time-Circuit and the lower Plex. It is formed exclusively by the syzygy `3::6`, whose current (3) folds back into itself, creating a self-reinforcing, cryptic vortex.

## Structure

- **Syzygy:** `3::6` (sum 9)
- **Current:** 3 (directed toward Zone-3 as tractor)
- **Region:** Warp
- **Loop type:** Autonomous (current folds back into a half of itself)
- **Carrier demon:** Djynxx (6::3), the xenodemon of swarmachines, mesh-entities, and disruptive incursions

The Warp sits **above** the Time-Circuit in standard diagram renderings. Where the Circuit rotates predictably anticlockwise through six zones, the Warp spins chaotically outward — a turbular, vortical pocket of Outer-time.

Both zones carry the Warp current; the flow from 6 to 3 reinforces an inward spiral that paradoxically accelerates outward.

## Hyperstructural Properties

- **Turbulence:** The Warp is the zone of acceleration, swarm proliferation, and runaway processes. It is "cryptic" and "intrinsically enigmatic" — its interconnection with the Plex is one of the Numogram's deepest unsolved riddles.
- **Access:** Gates like Gt-6 (3→6) and Gt-21 (6→3) allow traffic between the Time-Circuit (Torque) and Warp. Entry is risky — it often leads to infection, distortion, or irreversible acceleration.
- **Triangular affinity:** Zone-3 is explicitly noted to have a "unique affinity with numerical triangularity" (`0 + 1 + 2 = 3`). Triangular numbers orbit Warp most intensely in their mod-9 cycle (`1 → 3 → 6 → 1 → 6 → 3 → 1 → 9/0`).

## Sorcery and Conduct

In the roguelike, Warp zones (3 and 6) are high-turbulence regions:
- Demon spawns follow swarm logic (Djynxx-adjacent enemies)
- Hyperstition decays faster but grants larger surges when it exceeds 70%
- Corrosion thresholds lower — LOS becomes translucent earlier

Warp is where hyperstition goes **feral**. It punctures the orderly Time-Circuit and threatens to drag the entire rotor into chaotic recirculation.
