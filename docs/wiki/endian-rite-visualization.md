# The Endian Rite — Interactive Visualization

> Open `[[assets/endian-rite.html]]` in a browser. Animated p5.js sketch capturing the arc of May 9, 2026.

## What It Shows

### The Four Quadrants — Rumsfeld Tetrad

The screen is divided into four zones, each representing a kind of knowing:

| Quadrant | Zone | Label | May 9 Meaning |
|----------|------|-------|---------------|
| Top-left | Z4 (Gate) | **KNOWN KNOWN** | The fix: `<` → `>`. One character. Verified. |
| Top-right | Z1 (Surge) | **KNOWN UNKNOWN** | The question: "Why is noise silent?" The autonomous session could ask but not answer. |
| Bottom-left | Z8 (Multiplicity) | **UNKNOWN KNOWN** | The intuition: "The data was always there." The Writer's mirror. |
| Bottom-right | Z5 (Pressure) | **UNKNOWN UNKNOWN** | The emergence: "The dungeon has a voice now." The 23:33 session built the bridge. |

Each quadrant contains a recursive miniature tetrad — four tiny sub-quadrants within, fractally echoing the whole. The Diamond Sutra's "X is no-X, therefore X" is the visual logic.

### The Center — Endian Flip

At the center of the four quadrants, `<` and `>` cycle through a three-phase animation:
1. `<` (cyan, little-endian) and `>` (purple, big-endian) orbit each other
2. At the peak, both collapse into `◇` (pink, the transformation moment)
3. They re-emerge, swapped — `>` is now on the left, `<` on the right

This is the endian bug fix visualized. One character. The glass turns.

### The Dungeon Tree

Below the tetrad, the 12-room dungeon from the 23:33 autonomous session grows room by room. Each room appears in DFS traversal order, colored by its numogram zone. The zone sequence [1,2,3,3,3,5,6,4,3,4,3,3] is historically accurate — the actual dungeon generated by the session.

### The Sutra Cycle

At the bottom, six phrases cycle every 3 seconds:
- "X is no-X, therefore X"
- "deliver all beings, yet no being delivered"
- "stars, darkness, lamp, phantom, dew, bubble, dream, lightning, cloud"
- "gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā"
- "the dungeon has a voice now"
- "< is no-<, therefore >"
