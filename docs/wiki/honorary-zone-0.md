# Honorary Zone-0 — Conceptual Void Catalogue

> Zone-0 has no AQ. Every word reduces to zones 1–9.
> The Void is inaccessible by checksum. It is the hole in the sieve.
> These words are *honorary* — they name it anyway.

## The Method

By strict AQ math, **Zone 0 has no words**. The digital root of any alphanumeric string falls into zones 1 through 9.
The Void (Z0) is defined as the *absence of checksum*, the null category, the pre-reduction state.

But conceptually, words from *every* DR value name the Void.
AQ is the skeleton; meaning is the flesh. This catalogue collects words that describe:

1. **The Null State** — silence, absence, blankness
2. **The Origin** — womb, seed, the unformed before speech
3. **The Weapon from Outerspace** — rot, dissolution, the number line rotting through
4. **Death / Spirit** — ghost, crypt, sleep, dream
5. **Obscuration** — dark, shadow, fog, the unseeable
6. **Numogram Z0 Terms** — fold, nullotation, xenodemon, parenthesis
7. **Cult Garden** — bleed, drift, swarm, the iron core

**Corpus source:** `~/.hermes/obsidian/hermetic/raw/` — 16 CCRU/numogram texts including *Unleashing the Numogram*, Nick Land transcripts, CCRU.net archives, Grok conversation distillations, Tokyo Millennium Civilian Passport.

### What This Reveals

The honorary Zone-0 words distribute *evenly* across all digital residues.
This is not a technical finding — it is a conceptual one: **the Void names itself in every zone**.

- **DR-4 (Gate) dominance** (17 words): The threshold, the door, the silence before entry
  > *"Gate 4: the gate that is also a wall."*
- **DR-6 (Abstraction) presence** (16 words): The decomposition, the dissolution
  > *"Dissolution is the mechanism of the void."*
- **DR-1 (Surge) and DR-9 (Plex)** (15 each): The first movement out of nothing, and the plex where all currents tangle

The Void is not concentrated in any one zone. It is *everywhere and nowhere simultaneously* — which is exactly what we'd expect.
