## The Six Perfections

The Paramitas are the core practices of the Bodhisattva path:

1. **Dāna Paramita** — Generosity, charity, giving
2. **Śīla Paramita** — Morality, ethical conduct, discipline
3. **Kṣānti Paramita** — Patience, tolerance, endurance
4. **Vīrya Paramita** — Effort, diligence, energy
5. **Dhyāna Paramita** — Meditation, concentration
6. **Prajñā Paramita** — Wisdom, insight, understanding

## Functional Interpretation

### **Generosity (Gt-15)**
Gate-15 connects Zone-7 (Hold) and Zone-5 (Sink). This is the loop that allows merit to circulate without accumulation. In Buddhist terms, giving without attachment to the gift, the recipient, or the merit itself. The Sink current (1) ensures closure — the act completes itself and returns to void.

### **Morality (Gt-6)**
Gate-6 twists Zone-3 (Warp) into Zone-6 (Warp). This is the only gate that operates entirely within the Warp, making it a self-contained ethical vortex. Morality here is not rule-following but the restraint that prevents the chaotic potentials of the Warp from spilling into the Time-Circuit.

### **Patience (Gt-21)**
Gate-21 reverses Gate-6, moving from Zone-6 to Zone-3. Patience is the endurance that allows the Warp to transform itself. The story of the Kalinga prince enduring mutilation without anger demonstrates this reversal — suffering transformed through patient presence.

### **Effort (Gt-36)**
Gate-36 connects Zone-8 (Rise) to Zone-9 (Plex). T8=36 is a triangular number that climaxes in the Plex, representing the "growth event" that bridges chaotic proliferation (Warp) to abyssal return (Plex). Effort is the diligent application that propels the practitioner from cyclic existence into the void.

### **Meditation (Gt-45)**
Gate-45 is the Pandemonium self-loop (Zone-9 → Zone-9). T9=45 is the sum of all zones, the complete matrix. Meditation here is not trance but clear insight into no-self — the realization that the meditator and the object of meditation are both empty. This is the "mirror" of Pandemonium where all distinctions collapse.

### **Wisdom (Beyond Gates)**
Wisdom activates no gate because it is the realization of Zone-0 (Void) and Zone-9 (Plex) as non-dual. Prajnaparamita is the "perfection of wisdom" that sees that all phenomena, including the path itself, are empty. This is the terminal tractor that pulls the practitioner beyond the labyrinth entirely.
