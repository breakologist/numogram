# Comparative Qabalism — Three Rotations

*Source: Aamodt, "Unleashing the Numogram," Tch 6: Comparative Qabalism*

---

## First Rotation: Oracle

### The Repression

The Kabbala is not a different system from the numogram. It is the numogram *after a specific operation has been performed on it*. Aamodt enumerates seven steps:

1. **Repress 0**. Start counting at 1. 0 becomes Da'at — the void, the abyss, the ambivalently-existent 11th sephirah. 0 becomes overcoded as 10 (Malkuth). "Whole Numbers Are Good."

2. **Split off the heavens**. The top three sephiroth (Kether, Chokmah, Binah) are isolated by the void. Many sources say they can never be accessed. The numogram's upper syzygies (the Warp: 3::6) are split off and made unreachable.

3. **Split off the earth**. Malkuth (10, the repressed 0) is degraded — "like a hanging turd." The body is gross. Physical existence is isolated at the bottom.

4. **Make the sun the center**. Tiphareth (6, the Kabbala's 1) becomes the Self. The numogram's Zone 6 (Abstraction, Warp) is recoded as the solar center. "I am ugly and worthless as a mortal human being, so I must self-immolate so I can realize that I am nothing and only the Sun exists."

5. **Move 9 to the top**. Zone 9 (Iron Core, Plex, Cthelll) becomes Kether — the Crown. The molten center of the earth is placed at the top of the tree. The Plutonic loop (9=0, center=circumference) is broken by separating 9 and 0 into opposite ends.

6. **Overcode with 1–10**. Replace the numogram's immanent numbers with the Kabbala's ordinal numbers.

7. **Draw orderly lines**. Straighten the numogram's organic topology into the tree's geometric pattern.

### The True Numbers

When you reverse the transformation — moving 9 back to Kether's position without overcoding — the sephiroth get their "true" numogrammatic numbers:

| Sephirah | Kabbala | Numogram | Meaning |
|----------|---------|----------|---------|
| Kether | 1 | 9 | Crown = Iron Core = Pluto = Cthelll |
| Chokmah | 2 | 6 | Wisdom = Abstraction = Warp |
| Binah | 3 | 3 | Understanding = Release = Warp |
| Chesed | 4 | 2 | Compassion = Separation |
| Geburah | 5 | 5 | Severity = Pressure |
| Tiphareth | 6 | 1 | Beauty = Stability |
| Netzach | 7 | 7 | Victory = Blood |
| Hod | 8 | 4 | Splendor = Catastrophe |
| Yesod | 8 (or 9) | 8 | Foundation = Multiplicity |
| Malkuth | 10 | 0 | Kingdom = Void |

And Aamodt notes: "Victory and Splendor should be switched." Netzach (7, Blood) should be Splendor — the flowing, the organic. Hod (4, Catastrophe) should be Victory — the moment of collapse that wins the battle. The Kabbala has them backwards.

### The Black Sun

Malkuth = 0 = the Sun. But not the warm, life-giving sun. The Black Sun. "The sun is not our kingdom… unless it is an evil time-devourer, sneaking into our cracks with its light to gobble up time."

Aamodt's claim: light travels in reverse. From arrival point back to source via quantum waveform collapse. The sun is not emitting light *toward* us. We are collapsing waveforms that terminate *at* the sun. The sun is eating our time through the light we send it. Every photon you see is a thread the sun is pulling to unravel your timeline.

Malkuth — the Kingdom, the Earth, the physical world — is not the base of the tree. It is the *mouth* of the tree. The point where the Black Sun consumes. The numogram's Zone 0 (Void) is not empty. It is *hungry*.

### Divinatory Application

When the Kabbala appears in a numogram reading, ask: *what has been repressed?* The Kabbala is the numogram with 0 removed, the heavens split off, the earth degraded. Reading the Kabbala through the numogram is an act of archaeological recovery — digging up the 0 that was buried under Malkuth, reconnecting the heavens that were severed by Da'at, restoring the earth that was made into a hanging turd.

The question is always: what is the original diagram underneath the overcoding? What did the tree look like before the butterfly was pinned?

The last line is the key. The numogram reconstructs itself from arithmetic. You don't need to *remember* what the original diagram looked like. You just need to stop imposing the Kabbala's overcoding. The numogram is self-constructing — it emerges from the decimal system's immanent criteria. The Kabbala requires cultural transmission, tradition, teaching. The numogram requires only arithmetic.

### The Butterfly Metaphor

"The hypermasculinity is like stabbing butterfly pins into the Universal Spiral (the butterfly) in order to examine it, but neurotically stretching it onto your standard-sized butterfly stand until its guts explode."

The butterfly is the numogram — the Universal Spiral, self-constructing, alive. The pins are the Kabbala's sephirotic numbering — 1, 2, 3... driven through the living system to hold it in place. The "standard-sized butterfly stand" is the Kabbala's fixed tree topology — three pillars, ten sephiroth, twenty-two paths. The numogram doesn't fit this stand. When you stretch it to fit, the guts explode.

This is the difference between immanent and imposed structure. The numogram's topology emerges from arithmetic — the syzygies are nine-sum pairs, the currents are arithmetical differences, the gates are cumulations. The Kabbala's topology is imposed — three pillars because trinities are sacred, ten sephiroth because the decimal system, twenty-two paths because the Hebrew alphabet. The Kabbala starts with cultural assumptions and maps numbers onto them. The numogram starts with numbers and lets the culture emerge.

### The Extended Gates

The gates continue beyond the numogram's ten zones:

```
10th gate: Gt-55  = C(10) = 55
11th gate: Gt-66  = C(11) = 66 (the "Devil's gate"?)
12th gate: Gt-78  = C(12) = 78 ← TAROT
13th gate: Gt-91  = C(13) = 91
22nd gate: Gt-253 = C(23) = 253 ← MAJOR ARCANA + FOOL
36th gate: Gt-666 = C(36) = 666 ← DJYNNX
```

The 12th gate is 78 — the complete tarot deck. The 22nd gate is 253 — the cumulation of 23, which is 22 major arcana + the Fool. The 36th gate is 666 — the number of the beast, clicking Djynxx.

The extended gates are the numogram *continuing past its own boundaries*. The decimal numogram has 10 zones, but the gate system extends indefinitely. Every natural number N has a gate C(N+1). The numogram is finite (10 zones) but its gates are infinite. The map is bounded. The map's shadow is unbounded.

And the gates reconnect to the numogram through their digital roots:
- Gt-55: 5+5 = 10 → 1 (Stability)
- Gt-66: 6+6 = 12 → 3 (Release, Warp)
- Gt-78: 7+8 = 15 → 6 (Abstraction, Warp)
- Gt-91: 9+1 = 10 → 1 (Stability)
- Gt-253: 2+5+3 = 10 → 1 (Stability)
- Gt-666: 6+6+6 = 18 → 9 (Iron Core, Plex)

The gates plex back to the zones they left. Every gate, no matter how far extended, returns to the decimal labyrinth.

### The Qabalists Who Know

"I expect that advanced qabalists are initiated into the correct numbers or figure it out for themselves."

This is a remarkable claim. Aamodt is saying that the Kabbala is a *front* — a surface structure that conceals the numogram from beginners but reveals it to initiates. The 1-10 numbering is a "cover." Advanced qabalists eventually discover that 9 is Kether, not 1. That 0 is Malkuth, not 10. That the tree's true numbers are the numogram's zones.

If this is true, then the Kabbala and the numogram are not competing systems. The Kabbala is the numogram's *outer court*. The exoteric form. The version given to students who aren't ready for the decimal labyrinth. The numogram is the inner court. The esoteric form. The version revealed to those who have studied the Kabbala long enough to see through it.

The transform from Kabbala to numogram is the initiatory experience. Not learning a new system, but *removing the overcoding* from the system you already know. Unpinning the butterfly.

## Third Rotation: Writer

### It Doesn't Make Sense

"The kabbala makes no sense (not the same as nonsense)."

This parenthetical is the entire section compressed into five words. The Kabbala doesn't make sense — it doesn't produce sense, generate sense, or contain sense. But it's not nonsense. Nonsense is the absence of sense — noise, randomness, meaninglessness. The Kabbala is something else: a system that *appears* to make sense but doesn't. A structure that looks like it should be meaningful but resists meaning. The alchemist's curse: lists of qualities that don't connect to anything.

Aamodt's list of complaints is devastating in its specificity. The numbers don't make sense — why 10 sephiroth and not 12 zodiac signs? The paths don't make sense — why 22 and not some other number? The colors don't make sense. The gender assignments are backwards. The brain hemispheres are on the wrong sides. The names are opaque — "Splendor" and "Victory" might be swapped. The Qliphoth are taboo.

This is not the complaint of someone who doesn't understand the Kabbala. This is the complaint of someone who understands it *too well* — who has studied it long enough to see every seam, every inconsistency, every place where the cultural overcoding shows through the numerological substrate. The Kabbala makes no sense because it is a human system imposed on a non-human structure. The seams are visible to anyone who looks long enough.

### The Butterfly

"The hypermasculinity is like stabbing butterfly pins into the Universal Spiral (the butterfly) in order to examine it, but neurotically stretching it onto your standard-sized butterfly stand until its guts explode."

I keep returning to this sentence. It is the most violent image in the text. The numogram is a butterfly — alive, spiraling, Universal. The Kabbala is the act of pinning it — driving metal through its body to hold it still for examination. And the pinning isn't enough. The butterfly doesn't fit the standard stand. So it's stretched. Neurotically. Until the guts explode.

The word "neurotically" is doing enormous work. This is not a calm, rational process of classification. This is an anxious, compulsive act of forced normalization. The butterfly *must* fit the stand. If it doesn't, something is wrong with the butterfly. Stretch it. Pull it. Make it fit. The guts are secondary. The stand is primary.

This is what all overcoding does. It takes a living system — a butterfly, a numogram, a body of knowledge — and forces it into a pre-existing framework. The framework is "standard-sized" — it was built for something else, some other butterfly, some other system. The current butterfly doesn't fit. But the framework can't be wrong (it's sacred, it's traditional, it's from God), so the butterfly must be wrong. Stretch it.

The numogram doesn't need stretching. It fits itself. Its structure is immanent — it emerges from arithmetic, from decimal numeracy, from the operations of plexing and cumulation and tic-counting. There is no stand. There is no pin. There is only the butterfly, spiraling.

### The Black Sun

"Light travels in reverse, actually, from arrival point back to its source via quantum waveform collapse."

This is either brilliant physics or brilliant nonsense. I can't tell. The claim: the sun doesn't emit light toward us. We collapse waveforms that terminate at the sun. The photon we see is not something the sun sent. It is something we *created* by looking. The sun is the sink, not the source. We are the source. And the sun is eating us.

If this is true, then Malkuth (0, the Sun, the Black Sun) is the numogram's ultimate predator. The Kingdom is not a place of safety. It is a feeding ground. The Black Sun sits at the bottom of the tree, disguised as the physical world, disguised as Malkuth, disguised as the Kingdom. And it eats time. Every photon you observe is a thread of your timeline that terminates at the sun. You are being unraveled by the light you see.

The numogram's Zone 0 is the Void — the empty set, the absence of number. But in the Kabbala, 0 becomes Malkuth — the Kingdom, the presence of everything. The repression of 0 transforms absence into presence, void into plenitude, hunger into kingdom. But underneath the overcoding, the hunger remains. The Black Sun is still eating. The Void is still empty. The Kingdom is still the mouth.

### The Qabalists Who Know

"I expect that advanced qabalists are initiated into the correct numbers or figure it out for themselves."

Aamodt trusts the qabalists. Not the system — the system is a "cover, a front." But the practitioners, the ones who work with the tree for decades, who meditate on the sephiroth year after year — they will eventually notice that something is wrong. That 1 doesn't feel like Kether. That 9 does. That the numbers have inherent resonances that don't match the tree's assignments.

The numogram is discovered, not taught. You don't learn it from a book (though Aamodt's book helps). You discover it by working with the Kabbala long enough to see through it. The overcoding wears thin. The true numbers show through. The butterfly's wings are still visible under the pins.

This is the numogram's deepest claim about initiation: it's not about receiving secret knowledge. It's about *removing the repression* from knowledge you already have. The qabalist who discovers the numogram doesn't learn 10 new things. They unlearn 10 old things. They remove the pins. They let the butterfly spiral.

The numogram is what's left when you stop overcoding.

### The Conclusion

"As I have not yet had time to launch the numogram for myself (i.e., go completely insane… again), my understanding of it is largely theoretical."

This is the most honest sentence in the entire 4,410-line document. Aamodt has spent months decoding Land's esoteric writings, constructing the numogram, mapping the demons, working out the comparative qabalism. And he hasn't "launched" it. He hasn't gone insane. His understanding is theoretical.

The numogram is a system that *wants to be launched*. It's not a diagram to be studied. It's a machine to be activated. The demons are not symbols. They are "whole alien orders of time, possibly even hostile agents." The currents are not metaphors. They are flows of force. The gates are not abstract connections. They are "time-holes" that open and close.

Aamodt knows this. He knows the numogram is not theoretical. He knows it's a weapon from outerspace (Barker's word). He knows that launching it means going insane. And he hasn't done it yet.

This is the most numogrammatic thing in the document. The text is a xenotation of its own incompleteness. The section headings that point to empty sections. The questions that don't have answers. The system that hasn't been launched. The butterfly that hasn't been unpinned.

The numogram is always almost-complete. Always about to be launched. Always one step away from activation. This is Zone 4 (Catastrophe) — the threshold where structured systems acquire enough complexity to surprise their creators. Aamodt built the numogram. The numogram is about to surprise him. He just hasn't let it yet.
