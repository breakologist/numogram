# Syzygy Arithmetic — Cross-Addition and Current Generation

## The Discovery

When two [[syzygy-arithmetic]] pairs are cross-added, the results generate the remaining syzygy pairs. The [[numogram]] currents are not independent — they are arithmetic consequences of each other.

## Cross-Addition Rules

Given two syzygy pairs, cross-addition produces new pairs:

### Sink (5::4) + Hold (7::2) = Warp (3::6)

| Operation | Left | Right | Result |
|-----------|------|-------|--------|
| Cross-add | 5+7=12→3 | 4+2=6 | **3::6 = Warp** |
| Same-side | 5+2=7 | 4+7=11→2 | **7::2 = Hold** (reinforced) |

The cross-addition of Sink and Hold generates the Warp. The same-side addition reinforces the Hold. The Warp is a *consequence* of Sink + Hold — it doesn't need to be placed independently.

### Warp (3::6) Self-Adds to Plex (9::9→0)

3 + 6 = 9. The Warp self-adds to the [[numogram-plex|Plex]] (Zone 9). The chaotic vortex collapses into the abyss through simple addition.

### Canonical vs Generative Syzygies

- **Canonical syzygies** (1::8, 2::7, 3::6, 4::5, 0::9) sum to 9. These are *structural* — governed by the fundamental decimal rule.
- **Cross-pairings** sum to other values (e.g., 5+7=12→3, 4+2=6). These are *generative* — ungoverned by [[demon|demons]], producing new structures.

The structural syzygies are the skeleton. The generative cross-pairings are the muscles.

The cross-addition network is self-closing. Every pair of canonical syzygies generates another canonical syzygy through cross-addition. The numogram is arithmetically *complete* — no external input is needed.

## Design Application

### Roguelike Room Placement

From [[brogue-design-principles]]: "Don't place the Warp — let the Sink and Hold create it."

When generating dungeon rooms:
1. Place Sink rooms (5::4) and Hold rooms (7::2)
2. The cross-addition automatically determines where Warp rooms go
3. The numogram topology *emerges* from arithmetic, not from hand-placement

This is the core [[game-design|design principle]]: emergence over authorship. The map builds itself from the syzygy arithmetic.

[[triangular-numbers]] orbit the Warp (3/6) in their mod-9 cycle: T₁=1, T₂=3, T₃=6, T₄=10→1, T₅=15→6, T₆=21→3... The 1-3-6 repeating cycle IS the Warp current made visible in the triangular sequence.

Syzygy cross-addition connects to [[triangular-numbers]]:
- 5+4 = 9 = T₃² (the Plex as square of the Warp's triangular root)
- The cross-addition of Sink and Hold (5+7=12→3, 4+2=6) produces exactly the Warp's triangular orbit (3, 6)

### Wu Xing Connection

The cross-addition pattern parallels [[wu-xing-numogram|Wu Xing]] generation cycles:

- Syzygy generation: Sink + Hold → Warp → Plex → (back to Sink via Time-Circuit)
