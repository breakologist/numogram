# Wu Xing × Numogram: The Five Elements of the Decimal Labyrinth

## The Correspondence

The five Wu Xing elements map onto the five syzygy pairs of the numogram. Each element lives in a syzygy — the bond between two zones, not in a single zone.

| Element | Chinese | Syzygy | Zones | Carrier | Current | Character |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|---------|---------|-----------|
| Water | 水 (shuǐ) | 4::5 | Gate ↔ Pressure | Katak | 1 | Stillness, descent, the deep |
| Wood | 木 (mù) | 1::8 | Surge ↔ Multiplicity | Murrumur | 7 | Growth, expansion, branching |
| Fire | 火 (huǒ) | 2::7 | Separation ↔ Blood | Oddubb | 5 | Transformation, consumption |
| Metal | 金 (jīn) | 3::6 | Release ↔ Abstraction | Djynxx | 3 | Cutting, distillation, precision |
| Earth | 土 (tǔ) | 0::9 | Void ↔ Plex | Uttunul | 9 | Ground, center, the fold |

## The Generation Cycle (Shēng 生)

Wood → Fire → Earth → Metal → Water → Wood

Traced through the syzygies:
```
1::8 → 2::7 → 0::9 → 3::6 → 4::5 → 1::8
  (Wood)   (Fire)   (Earth)  (Metal)  (Water)   (Wood)
```

Each element "produces" the next through the syzygy chain. Wood (1::8, the Surge) gives rise to Fire (2::7, Separation-Blood). Fire's transformation yields Earth (0::9, the fold). Earth's stability enables Metal (3::6, the Warp cut). Metal's distillation produces Water (4::5, descent). Water nourishes Wood.

**The generation cycle is a pentagon** — each element connects to the next in sequence. 5 connections. The inner pentagon of the C(10)=45 pentagram.

## The Control Cycle (Kè 克)

Wood → Earth → Water → Fire → Metal → Wood

Traced through the syzygies:
```
1::8 → 0::9 → 4::5 → 2::7 → 3::6 → 1::8
  (Wood)   (Earth)  (Water)  (Fire)   (Metal)   (Wood)
```

Each element "overcomes" the element two steps ahead in the generation cycle. Wood (1::8) controls Earth (0::9) — roots break ground. Earth (0::9) controls Water (4::5) — banks contain rivers. Water (4::5) controls Fire (2::7) — quenching. Fire (2::7) controls Metal (3::6) — melting. Metal (3::6) controls Wood (1::8) — the axe fells the tree.

**The control cycle is a pentagram** — skip-one connections. 5 connections forming a star. The syzygy spokes of the C(10)=45 pentagram.

## The Pentagram Connection

The two Wu Xing cycles ARE the pentagram:

| Cycle | Pattern | Numogram Structure | Connections |
|-------|---------|-------------------|-------------|
| Generation (Shēng) | Pentagon | Inner pentagon edges | 5 |
| Control (Kè) | Pentagram | Syzygy spokes (skip-one) | 5 |

Together they form the complete interaction pattern of the five elements — 10 connections total. C(5,2) = 10. The decadence number. The Wu Xing's complete interaction space IS the decadence of the inner pentagon.

## Zone 5 as Earth's Shadow

Zone 5 (Pressure) doesn't participate in the syzygy elements — it's not part of any syzygy pair. But in the Wu Xing, Earth is sometimes placed at the CENTER of the diagram, separate from the four outer elements. Zone 5, sitting at the valley of the pentagram between zones 4 and 0, is the numogram's equivalent of this central Earth position.

In the Decadence game, 5+5=10 grants zero bonus. In the Wu Xing, the element at the center has no generating or controlling partner — it mediates between the other four. Zone 5 is the element that doesn't pair. The self-decadence is the center-Earth's refusal to participate in the cycles. It holds the system together by refusing to flow.

## Connection to the I Ching

The I Ching's 8 trigrams (bagua) each carry elemental associations:

| Trigram | Element | Binary | Zone (digital root) |
|---------|---------|--------|---------------------|
| ☰ Qián (Heaven) | Metal | 111 = 7 | DR 7 → Zone 7 |
| ☷ Kūn (Earth) | Earth | 000 = 0 | DR 0 → Zone 0 |
| ☳ Zhèn (Thunder) | Wood | 001 = 1 | DR 1 → Zone 1 |
| ☵ Kǎn (Water) | Water | 010 = 2 | DR 2 → Zone 2 |
| ☶ Gèn (Mountain) | Earth | 100 = 4 | DR 4 → Zone 4 |
| ☴ Xùn (Wind) | Wood | 110 = 6 | DR 6 → Zone 6 |
| ☲ Lí (Fire) | Fire | 011 = 3 | DR 3 → Zone 3 |
| ☱ Duì (Lake) | Metal | 111 = 7 | DR 7 → Zone 7 |

Wait — Heaven and Lake both map to binary 7. Let me use bottom-up reading (traditional):
| Trigram | Lines (bottom-up) | Binary | Decimal | Zone |
|---------|-------------------|--------|---------|------|
| ☰ Qián (Heaven) | 111 | 7 | 7 | Zone 7 (Metal ✓) |
| ☷ Kūn (Earth) | 000 | 0 | 0 | Zone 0 (Earth ✓) |
| ☳ Zhèn (Thunder) | 001 | 1 | 1 | Zone 1 (Wood ✓) |
| ☵ Kǎn (Water) | 010 | 2 | 2 | Zone 2 (Water? Fire?) |
| ☶ Gèn (Mountain) | 100 | 4 | 4 | Zone 4 (Earth?) |
| ☴ Xùn (Wind) | 110 | 6 | 6 | Zone 6 (Metal?) |
| ☲ Lí (Fire) | 011 | 3 | 3 | Zone 3 (Metal?) |
| ☱ Duì (Lake) | 111 | 7 | 7 | Zone 7 (Metal) |

The trigram-to-zone mapping via binary isn't clean for elemental correspondence. The I Ching's trigrams use a different encoding than the numogram's syzygy structure. The Wu Xing lives in the syzygies, not in the individual zones. The trigrams are a zone-level system; the elements are a syzygy-level system.

## The T'ai Hsuan Ching Counterpart

The T'ai Hsuan Ching (Book of the Great Dark) uses 81 tetragrams (3⁴) — a ternary system. The Wu Xing has 5 elements. 3 and 5 are the two odd primes that generate the numogram's structure (alongside 2). The T'ai Hsuan Ching's ternary basis (3) and the Wu Xing's quinary basis (5) together give the numogram's decimal basis: 3 × 5 = 15 (triangular number T(5)), and 2 × 5 = 10.

The I Ching is binary (2⁶). The T'ai Hsuan Ching is ternary (3⁴). The Wu Xing is quinary (5¹). The numogram is decimal (2×5). Each system captures one prime factor of 10. The numogram is the synthesis — the system where all three primes converge.

## The Forgotten Element?

In some Chinese traditions, a sixth element exists — Soil (as distinct from Earth), or sometimes Void. If the numogram's Wu Xing mapping is complete at 5 syzygies, what about Zone 5's self-decadence? Zone 5 (Pressure) is the numogram's "sixth element" — the one that doesn't pair, the one at the center, the one that holds the others together by refusing to participate. In the Wu Xing, the central position is often left empty or assigned to the Emperor (the mediator). Zone 5 is the Emperor. 5+5=10 is the Emperor speaking to himself.
