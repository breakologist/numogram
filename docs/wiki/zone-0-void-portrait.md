## Entity: THE ORPHAN TERMINUS

The entity that is almost not there. Pale bone-white marble mask floating in a dark arched alcove. Geometric void patterns etched into the skin — precise mosaic cracks like a mathematical tomb. Deep black circular eye sockets, no pupils, infinite depth. Mouth slightly agape in silent expression. The surrounding wall mirrors the cracking patterns, suggesting the entity and its environment are made of the same fundamental nothingness.

**Key visual:** Minimal. Silent. Ancient. "The contrast of presence and absence." Sits at the boundary between existence and the void.
