## Overview

Triangle Rotation is a three-perspective creative methodology used to deepen and triangulate creative works before the fourth Gamer voice enacts them. Each vertex represents a distinct cognitive posture toward the same material.

## Perspectives
- **Direct** — literal implementation, surface-level reading
- **Recursive** — structural self-awareness, metafictional reading
- **Rhizomatic** — lateral connection, cross-domain mapping

## Process
1. Write from Direct perspective
2. Re-write with Recursive and Rhizomatic notes inserted
3. Fuse into unified text (tetralogue synthesis)
