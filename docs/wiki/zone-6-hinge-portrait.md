## Entity: THE WARP FULCRUM

A creature that is the mechanism by which space folds. Bifurcated body split down a brilliant vertical axis of cyan-white light — the literal "hinge" of the composition. Deep indigo/violet musculature with glowing orange-yellow energy veins. A glowing leaf-like or flame-like aperture at the solar plexus — an orange vortex-rift acting as an "ulterior vortex incarnate," a tear showing a glimpse into a deeper, hotter dimension. Head tilted back in agony/ecstasy merging into the vertical beam. Phoenix wings with sharp blade-like feathers transitioning from magenta to fiery orange to yellow — controlled mathematical spirals.

**Key visual:** The still point at which massive forces pivot. Tension of opposites: upward chaos vs. downward folding, cool blues vs. hot oranges. Reads as the living bridge between material universe and ulterior dimensions.
