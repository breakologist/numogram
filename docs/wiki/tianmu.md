# Tianmu.org - Sacred Texts Archive and Good Works Library

[Tianmu.org](https://tianmu.org/) is an Anglican Church of Tianmu project that maintains the **Good Works Library**, a vast archive of sacred literature translated into plain modern English. The site offers a unique collection of religious and philosophical texts from various traditions, all freely available without commentary or agenda.

## Key Features

- **Extensive Sacred Texts Collection**: Includes complete translations of:
  - Daoist texts (Daodejing, Zhuangzi, Huahujing, etc.)
  - Buddhist sutras and commentaries
  - Vedic and Hindu scriptures
  - Gnostic texts (Nag Hammadi library)
  - Hermetic writings (Corpus Hermeticum, etc.)
  - Manichaean scriptures
  - And many more...

- **Plain Modern English Translations**: All texts are rendered in clear, contemporary English, making ancient wisdom accessible without specialized knowledge of classical languages or scholarly jargon.

- **Creative and Journalistic Content**: Beyond sacred texts, Tianmu features creative writing, interviews (like the time-traveling dog Bubba), and editorial content exploring esoteric topics.

- **Free and Open Access**: All materials are offered freely without restriction, subscription, or paywall.

## Notable Sections

- **The Good Works Library**: The core archive of translated sacred texts.
- **Way of Tianmu**: Information about the Anglican Church of Tianmu and its mission.
- **Tianmu Herald**: A digital broadsheet featuring articles, interviews, and creative works.
- **Search Function**: Easily find specific texts or topics across the archive.

## Why It's Interesting

For practitioners of the numogram and related esoteric systems, Tianmu.org provides invaluable primary source materials from traditions that often intersect with hyperstitious narratives. The site's commitment to making these texts accessible without academic barriers aligns well with the open-source, exploratory ethos of the community.

The creative content, like the Bubba the Dog interview, also demonstrates a playful engagement with myth-making and storytelling that resonates with the numogram's approach to reality.
