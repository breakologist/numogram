# The Pandemonium Matrix

## Structure

C(10,2) = 45 demons. Each demon is a connection between two zones — the "connective tissue" of the numogram. Remove the demons and the numogram becomes ten isolated points.

### Three Families

| Family | Count | Formula | Zones Connected | Character |
|--------|-------|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Chronodemons | 15 | C(6,2) | Time Circuit {1,8,2,7,5,4} internal | Internal connections, corridors |
| Amphidemons | 24 | 6×4 | Time Circuit ↔ Outer {3,6,0,9} | Bridges, portals, cross-circuit |
| Xenodemons | 6 | C(4,2) | Outer {3,6,0,9} among themselves | Peripheral, atonal, unpredictable |

### Five Syzygetic Demons (Null Pitch)

These fall on syzygy lines (sum to 9). They register as silence in the pitch system.

| Syzygy | Name | Mesh | Type | Notes |
|--------|------|------|------|-------|
| 0::9 | Uttunul | 36 | Xenodemon | Plex carrier, closes the numogram |
| 1::8 | Murrumur | 28 | Chronodemon | First pair in Time Circuit |
| 2::7 | Oddubb | 20 | Chronodemon | Middle pair in Time Circuit |
| 3::6 | Djynxx | 12 | Xenodemon | Warp carrier, links Warp to Warp |
| 4::5 | Katak | 00 | Chronodemon | Sink, at the bottom, pulls everything down |

### Four Decadence Pairs (Sum to 10)

These are NOT syzygies (don't sum to 9) but share a structural kinship.

| Pair | Family | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1::9 | Amphidemon | Surge reaches to Plex |
| 2::8 | Amphidemon | Separation reaches to Multiplicity |
| 3::7 | Amphidemon | Release reaches to Blood |
| 4::6 | Amphidemon | Gate reaches to Abstraction |

All four decadence pairs are amphidemons — they cross the circuit/outer boundary. The self-decadence 5+5=10 is zone 5 connecting to itself (the phase-change discussed in the fiveness tetralogue).

## Mesh-Serial Ordering (Canonical — from CCRU Writings 1997-2003 + Vexsys)

### Five Demon Types (Corrected)

| Type | Count | Character |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Amphidemon | 24 | Bridges, portals, cross-circuit connections |
| Cyclic Chronodemon | 12 | Time Circuit internal, patrol loops |
| Syzygetic Chronodemon | 3 | Katak (5::4), Oddubb (7::2), Murrumur (8::1) — null pitch, structural |
| Chaotic Xenodemon | 4 | Ixix (3::0), Tchu (6::0), Ununuttix (9::3), Unnutchi (9::6) |
| Syzygetic Xenodemon | 2 | Djynxx (6::3), Uttunul (9::0) — Warp and Plex carriers |

*Note: The triangle rotation's three-family classification (15 chronodemons, 24 amphidemons, 6 xenodemons) was an approximation. The actual system has five types. The 15 "chronodemons" split into 12 Cyclic + 3 Syzygetic Chronodemons. The 6 "xenodemons" split into 4 Chaotic + 2 Syzygetic Xenodemons. The 24 amphidemons remain unchanged.*

Each demon "clicks" a gate-number (Gt-N) and has a pitch (Ana-N descending to Cth-N). The mesh-serial is the Subdecadence game's negative score threshold that triggers the demon call.
