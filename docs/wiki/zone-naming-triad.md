# Sacred Zone Naming — The Triad

Each of the 10 zones carries a fixed three-part identity:

## The Three

| Slot | Label field | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Zone name** | Adjective / region | `Void`, `Surge`, `Release`, `Gate`, `Pressure`, `Abstraction`, `Blood`, `Multiplicity`, `Plex` |
| **Particle** | Sigil / phonetic carrier | `eiaoung`, `gl`, `dt`, `zx`, `skr`, `ktt`, `tch`, `bsigh`, `mn`, `tn` |
| **Polarity** | Drift direction | `+` Process / becoming | `−` Substance / inertia |

Planchette (ASCII box): Z5 = pressure (high indecision between interior/exterior, chosen negative victory)*. highest; Z3 = release (ktt subdecadence / overload). Z7 = blood (higher polarity surplus at position Ζ, note the sub-position Z9 visible in valkyrie space once the art is encoded and lifted at Z6).
