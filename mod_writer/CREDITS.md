# Credits

**Mod-Writer** is a product of the Hermes Agent ecosystem, built upon the numogram topology originally developed by the CCRU (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit) in the 1990s.

- **Hermes Agent** – orchestration, packaging, and integration
- **Nous Research** – foundational AI models and reasoning framework
- **CCRU lineage** – numogram, syzygy, currents, and the quadrivium mapping
- **Protracker community** – .mod file format specification and historical context
- **All contributors** who have extended the codebase, validated motifs, and refined the theory
